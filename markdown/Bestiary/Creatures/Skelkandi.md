A salamander-like creature that lives within the [[Ergantine Lake]]. It bears decades to centuries of exposure to [[Creep]].

Nearly 40 yards long, its mouth alone is wide enough to swallow a [[Teetle Cart]] with room to spare. Has only dared to attack [[The Land of Tidwell/World Details/Nations & States/Forestdown/Gatehouse/Gatehouse]] once, at a time when the majority of its forces were far to the east to confront the [[The Land of Tidwell/World Details/Nations & States/The Waste/Varjalune Republic/Varjalune Republic]] over their declining tribute via the [[Andam]]. This suggests some amount of intelligence in the creature.

The attack lead to the collapsed northernmost tower of the [[Eskikale Keep]].
