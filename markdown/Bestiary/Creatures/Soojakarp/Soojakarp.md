A massive shelled creature often covered in an ecosystem of its own. Soojakarp consume the [[Sallidean Stone]] that propagates in the [[Vaiknelaht Bay]].
Corals and smaller sea life are attracted to soojakarp due to their extremely sloppy eating habits. Thus, soojakarp are reliable, living fish beds and serve as the basis for successful fisheries on the Living soojakarp are an extremely valuable commodity in the [[The Land of Tidwell/World Details/Nations & States/The Waste/Varjalune Republic/Varjalune Republic]], with wealthy families often having between one to five of these in family fisheries.
