A large, flat-headed cave creature. Possesses two long, thin, whip-like forelimbs segmented at its front. It has a broad, saw-like mandible and several dull crusty eyes. It is of an order of creatures that survive by "ranching," they mind and control populations of another population of creatures carefully, picking off only those that it needs to sustain itself, otherwise providing crude subsistence means for those creatures to survive.
