Massive shelled filter feeders that creep along the canyons and surfaces of the [Gyhrra Badlands](onenote:#Gyhrra%20Badlands&section-id={28F7D53B-5AAC-0544-B9A9-39509EA17203}&page-id={B671430F-BF32-40F2-BE0E-6EF4C82D8DA5}&end&base-path=https://d.docs.live.net/f01a5f00181e1dce/Documents/Wizzarrd/Story%20Details.one). These creatures are sometimes killed for sport by big game hunters from Risbullah, but the industry is tightly regulated after they were nearly hunted to extinction in past ages. They support the majority of the remaining ecosystem of the [Gyhrra Badlands](onenote:#Gyhrra%20Badlands&section-id={28F7D53B-5AAC-0544-B9A9-39509EA17203}&page-id={B671430F-BF32-40F2-BE0E-6EF4C82D8DA5}&end&base-path=https://d.docs.live.net/f01a5f00181e1dce/Documents/Wizzarrd/Story%20Details.one), as their deaths sustain the numerous carrion creatures upon whose young they feed while alive.  
  
The carapaces of these creatures form the major structures at the center of [Risbullah](onenote:#Risbullah&section-id={28F7D53B-5AAC-0544-B9A9-39509EA17203}&page-id={CFFE9A3C-7EAB-4F3D-8122-D91E992CF37E}&end&base-path=https://d.docs.live.net/f01a5f00181e1dce/Documents/Wizzarrd/Story%20Details.one), though who assembled them is a mystery.
