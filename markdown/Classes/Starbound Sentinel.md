### Class Items

_A Hygiane Order field badge you may or may not have earned_ or _an instrument for measuring Creep density that hums near Etherotaxia_

### Hope Feature

_Manifest Protection_: Spend 3 Hope. When you activate The Ward, it also immediately deals d6 magic damage using your Proficiency to all Creep-touched or Etherotaxia adversaries within Close range.

### Class Feature

**The Ward**
Once per rest, activate The Ward as an action. Until the end of the scene, you project a protective field:

- Allies within Close range cannot be directly targeted by effects originating from the Creep, Etherotaxia, or haunts of Forestdown. They can still be affected by area effects.
- When an adversary bearing Creep corruption or Etherotaxia targets you specifically, gain a bonus to your Evasion equal to your Spellcast Trait.
  You may freely deactivate and reactivate The Ward during the scene.

# Subclasses

## **Threshold Keeper**

_Play the Threshold Keeper if you want to be the boundary between what your allies face and what they should never have to face._

### Spellcast Trait

Agility

### Foundation Features

_Hold the Line_: Once per rest, while The Ward is active, if an ally within Close range would mark their last Hit Point from a Creep or Etherotaxia source, they mark a Stress instead, and you mark 1 Stress.

_Fearless Stand_: While The Ward is active, you project a suppressive [[Aura]] (Maintenance Difficulty 13). At the end of each of your turns, each ally within Very Close range is shielded once from the next condition or harmful effect applied to them by a Creep or Etherotaxia source before the start of your next turn. If you are hit while maintaining this aura and fail the Maintenance roll, the aura collapses until the start of your next turn.

### Specialization Feature

**Warden's Challenge**: Once per scene, while The Ward is active, activate a challenging [[Aura]] (Maintenance Difficulty 17). While this aura is active, any adversary within Close range must target you rather than any other character. If you are hit and fail the Maintenance roll, the aura collapses immediately.

### Mastery Feature

**Unbroken Vigil**: Once per session, expand The Ward to Far range instead of Close range for the entirety of a scene. During this time, you cannot take actions that don't directly protect an ally; all your focus is on the boundary you're holding.

## **Rift-Touched**

_Play the Rift-Touched if you want to understand what you guard against well enough to turn it back and eventually wield it._

### Spellcast Trait

Knowledge

### Foundation Features

_Counter-Resonance_: While The Ward is active, you can sense the nature of Creep manifestations and Etherotaxia in use within Far range without a roll. The GM describes what you detect in broad terms — enough to act on, not enough to fully understand.

_Absorbed Strangeness_: Once per rest, when you successfully protect an ally from a Creep or Etherotaxia effect using any ability, gain 1 Hope and add +2 to your next Spellcast Roll, as the redirected energy sharpens your attunement.

### Specialization Feature

**Turn the Weird**: Once per scene, redirect one Creep or Etherotaxia effect targeting an ally you are protecting. That effect now targets an adversary of your choice within Close range instead. Make a Spellcast Roll (14). On a success, the effect is fully transferred. On a failure, it dissipates harmlessly.

### Mastery Feature

**The Carried Weight**: Once per session, absorb a powerful Creep or Etherotaxia effect entirely into yourself, preventing it from affecting anyone else in the scene. You mark 3 Stress, but the effect is fully nullified. Something from Forestdown leaves a mark on you. Work with your GM to determine what changes.

## Mechanical Notes

The Ward needs to be active before the Creep or Etherotaxia reach your allies — raise it at the first sign of supernatural involvement, not after someone has already been targeted. The Threshold Keeper path is built around holding position. Stack Fearless Stand and Warden's Challenge to concentrate all incoming threat on yourself, but be ready for the Maintenance rolls to fail under sustained pressure. Hold the Line is your emergency release valve so don't spend it too early. The Rift-Touched path rewards attentiveness. Counter-Resonance gives you advance information every turn The Ward is up, so use it to actively plan. Absorbed Strangeness fires once per rest and stacks a +2 onto your next Spellcast Roll — save that roll for Turn the Weird or a high-stakes moment. The Carried Weight is a session-defining sacrifice and should be treated as a last resort.
