### Class Items

_A heavy chain wrap or collar_ or _an ornamental tabard bearing a symbol_

### Hope Feature

_Proud Defense_: Spend 3 Hope to reduce the incoming damage.

### Class Features

**Flashy Display**
When you damage an adversary, you can mark a Stress to give them the _Enthralled_ condition. An _Enthralled_ creature can only attack you. This condition lasts until the GM spends a number of Fear equal to your Spellcast trait to remove it or you damage the creature again.

**Hide Before Honor**
After a long rest, you can choose to give yourself the _Honorbound_ condition. While _Honorbound_, you gain a penalty on all rolls equal to your Tier. Allies within Far range can take a bonus equal to your Tier on all rolls.

# Subclasses

## **Pillar Beast**

_Play the Pillar Beast if you want to use your prowess in martial matters to boost the abilities of your allies._

### Spellcast Trait

Agility

### Foundation Features

_Diverting Blow_: After you successfully attack an enemy, you can make a Spellcast Roll against them. On a success, their next attack roll receives a -2 penalty.
_Social Parachute_: You can use your Agility to make a strategic retreat from a social situation. Once per rest, you can make a Spellcast Roll against a target with whom you or an ally is speaking. On a success, you and an ally within Very Close range may exit the interaction unnoticed.

### Specialization Feature

**Pounce**: When an ally within Close range is attacked, you can make a Spellcast Roll against the attacker. On a success, you move to the attacker's location and the attacker gains a -3 penalty on the attack roll.

### Mastery Feature

**Unbreakable Line**: Once per scene, when you intercept an attack with **Pounce** or your positioning features, reduce the attack's damage by your Tier before it is applied.

## **Hidewarden**

_Play the Hidewarden if you're looking to put yourself deep into bad situations to gain benefits in self-sacrifice._

### Spellcast Trait

Instinct

### Foundation Features

_My Body is a Shield_: When you take damage from an attack, you can spend a Hope and gain a +2 bonus to your damage threshold for every Hit Point you currently have marked.
_Power Through_: Once per scene, mark a stress to clear a condition on you. If you clear a condition in this manner, gain Advantage on your next roll.

### Specialization Feature

**Legendary Flex**: Once per session when you have one or more marked Armor Slots, you can spend 2 Hope to clear one Armor slot. When you clear an Armor Slot in this manner, your next success with Hope during this scene counts as a Critical Success.

### Mastery Feature

**Last to Fall**: The first time each session you would mark your last Hit Point, mark 2 Stress instead and remain at 1 unmarked Hit Point. You immediately gain 1 Hope.

## Mechanical Notes

You have two class features. Decide during each rest whether to activate Hide Before Honor as the trade of your own roll penalty for an ally bonus is significant and shouldn't be a passive afterthought. Flashy Display costs a Stress, so use it on the most dangerous adversary in a scene to lock their targeting onto you. The Pillar Beast path plays as a mobile interceptor. Pounce is a reaction that repositions you and penalizes the attacker, so position yourself where you can reach the most likely attack targets. Diverting Blow stacks on top of normal attacks for free. The Hidewarden path rewards absorbing punishment. My Body is a Shield becomes stronger the more Hit Points you have marked, so don't treat damage as something to avoid. Last to Fall is a guaranteed once-per-session survival moment.
