### Class Items

_A vestment, badge, or symbol of an office you hold or claim_ or _a personal affectation that reads as authoritative to anyone who sees it_

### Hope Feature

_The Voice Carries_: Spend 3 Hope. Until the end of the scene, your Sacred Address activates automatically whenever you make a successful Presence roll. The scene use is not consumed.

### Class Feature

**Sacred Address**
Once per scene, when you speak to a group of at least two characters, your words carry both social weight and divine resonance. Choose one effect:

- All characters who hear you gain a +1 bonus to Attitude toward you or toward a cause you name, lasting until the end of the scene.
- All allies within Far range who can hear you clear 1 Stress.
- One adversary who hears you gains the _Shamed_ condition until their next action. _Shamed_ characters gain a -2 penalty to their Difficulty.

### Background Questions

_Answer any of the following background questions. You can also create your own questions._

- Who first told you that people listened to you differently than they listened to others? Were they right?
- Do you believe what you say, or have you said it long enough that the question doesn't matter anymore?
- What movement or community formed around your words, and how do you feel about what it became?

### Connections

_Ask your fellow players one of the following questions for their characters to answer, or create your own questions._

- You've heard me speak when I didn't know anyone was listening. What did I say?
- I convinced you of something once that you later questioned. What was it?
- You've previously met someone who reminded you of me, and it unsettled you. Who were they?

# Subclasses

## **The Congregation's Marshal**

_Play the Congregation's Marshal if you want to lead through loyalty, building the network of people who will move when you speak._

### Spellcast Trait

Presence

### Foundation Features

_Rally the Faithful_: Once per rest, when you use Sacred Address, you may double its effective range. If you do, the chosen effect (excluding _Shame_) applies to all characters who can hear you, ally or not.

_Commanding Bearing_: Whenever you succeed on a Presence roll with Hope, you may immediately designate one character present who gains a +2 bonus to Attitude toward you until the next scene.

### Specialization Feature

**Standard-Bearer**: Once per scene, when an ally within Far range fails a roll, you can call out to them. They reroll with a +2 bonus added to the result. They must use the new result.

### Mastery Feature

**The March**: Once per session, when you use Sacred Address, choose one goal you describe aloud. Every GMC who heard you will take one meaningful action toward that goal before the end of the session, provided it doesn't require them to act entirely against their nature.

## **The Touched Preacher**

_Play the Touched Preacher if you want your words to carry something more than persuasion, something your party will feel when you speak._

### Spellcast Trait

Presence

### Foundation Features

_Laying On_: Once per rest, as part of your class feature or as an independent action, touch a willing character. They clear 2 Hit Points or 2 Stress (your choice).

_Marked Flock_: Once per rest, when you use Sacred Address, choose up to two allies within Very Close range. Those allies gain a +1 bonus to all rolls until the end of the scene.

### Specialization Feature

**Channel the Belief**: Once per scene, when an ally within Close range would mark their last Hit Point, spend 2 Hope. They mark a Stress instead, and you clear a Stress.

### Mastery Feature

**Consecrated Ground**: Once per session, designate the current location as sacred. Until the end of the session, allies within Very Close range of that location cannot be affected by fear-based effects as long as they remain within.

## Mechanical Notes

You are strongest in crowd scenes and multi-character social encounters. Position yourself centrally and speak first. Sacred Address before a social roll sets up your whole group for success. You have real power in combat too, but look for moments where the social and tactical overlap (demoralizing an adversary while rallying allies). The Marshal path builds influence that compounds over a session; the Preacher path focuses on keeping your party in fighting shape moment to moment.
