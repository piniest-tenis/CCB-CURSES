### Class Items

_A sigil of your god_ or _a set of tools for your chosen art_

### Hope Feature

_Perseverance_: Spend 3 Hope to clear 2 Stress on yourself or an ally within Close range.

### Class Feature

**Inspiration**
Once per scene, when you or an ally within Far range makes a roll, you can spend 1 Hope to inspire them. Before the roll, choose one benefit:

- Gain a +2 bonus to the result.
- On a success, clear 1 Stress.
- On a critical success, clear 1 Hit Point.

### Subclasses

As a Devout, choose between the following subclasses.

**Impactful Artist**
_Play the Impactful Artist if you want to influence entire societies by appealing to their entire populace._

**True Believer**
_Play the True Believer if you want to wield your faith to garner yourself immense political power via ruthless cunning._

### Background Questions

_Answer any of the following background questions. You can also create your own questions._

- Who or what is your deity, and why? What has led you to follow this particular ideology?
- How did you first realize you were able to influence others' thoughts and opinions?
- What do you do in order to show your devotion to your beliefs?

### Connections

_Ask your fellow players one of the following questions for their characters to answer, or create your own questions._

- Do my beliefs bring you comfort or make you uncomfortable? How does that affect our relationship?
- Were you familiar with my chosen artform before we were introduced? What was your opinion of it?
- You've asked me to do a favor for you if you were to not survive, but you're worried I won't follow through. What favor is it and why are you worried?

# Subclasses

## **Impactful Artist**

_Play the Impactful Artist if you want to influence entire societies by appealing to their entire populace._

### Spellcast Trait

Presence

### Foundation Features

_Paean_: Once per long rest, you can deliver a performance of your most famous work. All allies within Far range can use your Presence score as their own for one roll until the end of the Scene.
_Divine Gift_: After you make an action roll to influence someone, you can spend any number of Hope to add that many d4s to the result of the roll.

### Specialization Feature

**Seminal Works**: Your most influential art has garnered you respect amongst a [[Faction]] of your choosing. Gain a +1 bonus to the [[Attitude]] of all [[Adherent|Adherents]] of that faction.

### Mastery Feature

**Masterpiece of Conviction**: Once per session, spend 2 Hope when you complete a major public performance. Choose up to three allies within Far range. Each ally clears 1 Stress and gains Advantage on their next Presence roll.

## **True Believer**

_Play the True Believer if you want to wield your faith to garner yourself immense political power via ruthless cunning._

### Spellcast Trait

Presence

### Foundation Features

_Blistering Sermon_: Once per long rest, you can invoke your deity's name in proselytizing to others. All adversaries within Far range take a -1 penalty to their Difficulty.
_Righteous Indignation_: When you make a roll to influence a target within Far who has a negative [[Attitude]] towards you, you can mark any number of Stress. For each 2 Stress marked, the target gains a +1 bonus to [[Attitude]] for your next interaction.

### Specialization Feature

**Polemic**: You have developed the ability to overwhelm others with your personal devotion. Once per long rest, you can give a character the _Stunned_ condition simply by talking to them. While _Stunned_, a character cannot make action or reaction rolls. This condition is cleared only when you stop talking to them.

### Mastery Feature

**Immutable Creed**: When a Character attempts to coerce or magically compel you, mark 1 Stress to ignore that effect and gain 1 Hope. Once per scene, an ally who hears your refusal may also clear 1 Stress.

## Mechanical Notes

Inspiration is flexible support: choose the benefit before the roll lands. The bonus to result is reliable; the Stress-clear option works best when allies are already under pressure; the Hit Point option is worth saving for moments where critical success is genuinely likely. The Impactful Artist path scales with Hope investment. Build your Hope pool intentionally and spend it on the influence rolls that matter most. The True Believer path is strongest when you have multiple adversaries or difficult attitude targets to work against; Blistering Sermon and Righteous Indignation both reward acting before the party's key social rolls rather than reacting to outcomes. Both paths are strongest when you're in reach of your allies and acting first.
