### Class Items

_A symbol of your clandestine cause_ or _a stack of pamphlets about your cause_

### Hope Feature

_The Meaning Behind It_: Spend 3 Hope. Until the end of the scene, every action you take in service of your cause provides all three benefits from Cover and Cause simultaneously.

### Class Feature

**Cover and Cause**
Once per scene, name the cause you are serving in this moment. When you take any action directly advancing that cause (through deception, sabotage, or open heroism), choose one benefit:

- Gain Advantage on the action's roll.
- An ally within Far range who can see your action gains +2 to their next roll this scene.
- Gain 1 Hope.

### Subclasses

As a Banner Saboteur, choose between the following subclasses.

**The Shadow Patriot**
_Play the Shadow Patriot if you want to fight for something real while working through means no one will ever be able to confirm were yours._

**The Vanguard Deceiver**
_Play the Vanguard Deceiver if you want your courage to be the truth and your deceptions to be the method, with your allies feeling the difference._

### Background Questions

_Answer any of the following background questions. You can also create your own questions._

- What is the cause you serve, and when did you decide it was worth lying for?
- What is the line you won't cross even in service of it?
- Who on the other side knows your real identity, and what prevents them from using it against you?

### Connections

_Ask your fellow players one of the following questions for their characters to answer, or create your own questions._

- You know what I actually serve, and you've kept it quiet. Why?
- I lied to you on behalf of the cause. Have you forgiven me?
- You've been asked to vouch for me as cover. Did it cost you anything?

# Subclasses

## **The Shadow Patriot**

_Play the Shadow Patriot if you want to fight for something real while working through means no one will ever be able to confirm were yours._

### Spellcast Trait

Presence

### Foundation Features

_The Handler_: Once per rest while operating under a false identity in service of your cause, gain Advantage on all Presence rolls to maintain that identity.

_Operational Security_: When you use Cover and Cause, the action you take cannot be traced back to your real identity unless you choose to let it be.

### Specialization Feature

**Turn the Asset**: Once per scene, identify a GMC currently working for an adversary [[Faction]]. Make a Presence roll (14). On a success, you reveal information that convinces them to take one action against that faction this session. They believe the decision was entirely their own.

### Mastery Feature

**Deep Cover**: Once per session, spend a long rest establishing a complete false identity in a new location or with a new faction. Future rolls to maintain this identity are made with Advantage. The identity persists between sessions until actively dismantled by you or via discovery.

## **The Vanguard Deceiver**

_Play the Vanguard Deceiver if you want your courage to be the truth and your deceptions to be the method, with your allies feeling the difference._

### Spellcast Trait

Presence

### Foundation Features

_Rally by Deception_: When you use Cover and Cause to benefit an ally, they also gain a +1 bonus to their Evasion until the end of the scene.

_Righteous Misdirection_: Once per rest, when an adversary targets an ally instead of you, you can intervene as a reaction and redirect their attention using a taunt or misdirecting action. Make a Presence roll (12). On a success, the interaction redirects to you. Can be used socially or in combat.

### Specialization Feature

**The Cause Leads**: Once per scene, when you directly protect an ally in combat or through social misdirection, gain 1 Hope. If you do so while under a false identity, gain 2 Hope instead.

### Mastery Feature

**Stand and Be Counted**: Once per session, reveal your true identity in a dramatic moment. All adversaries who believed your cover must mark 2 Stress at the revelation. All allies within Far range gain Advantage on their next roll. After this, your cover for this session is burned, but you gain +2 to all rolls for the rest of the session.

## Mechanical Notes

Cover and Cause requires you to name your cause at the scene level. Don't assume it carries over automatically. The clearer you are about what you're serving in this moment, the more easily the GM can confirm when an action qualifies for the benefit. The Shadow Patriot path is for players who want to win through accumulated social manipulation and information control; the Vanguard Deceiver path is for players who want to protect allies through misdirection and tactical self-sacrifice. Both paths benefit from knowing which faction or cause you're advancing before the scene begins.
