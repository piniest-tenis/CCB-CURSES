### Class Items

_Scars or natural weapons that have been maintained and used_ or _a weapon that has marks of repeated repair_

### Hope Feature

_Hunt's End_: Spend 3 Hope. Your next attack against your Prey is made with Advantage. On a Critical Success, they take double damage and gain the _Slowed_ condition until the end of their next turn.

### Class Feature

**Blood Scent**
Once per scene, mark a target as your Prey. While a creature is your Prey, your attacks against them deal +1 damage, and if they attempt to create distance from you, they mark a Stress. You can only designate one Prey at a time. Designating a new Prey immediately releases the previous one.

# Subclasses

## **The Houndtooth**

_Play the Houndtooth if you want to end fights through inevitability by tracking, cornering, and closing in until there's nowhere left to go._

### Spellcast Trait

Instinct

### Foundation Features

_Chase Instinct_: When your Prey moves away from you, you may immediately move up to Very Close range as a reaction without spending your action. This cannot place you in immediate danger beyond the act of pursuit.

_Sense the Wound_: When your Prey has marked at least one Hit Point, you gain Advantage on all rolls against them.

### Specialization Feature

**Relentless Tracking**: Once per rest, when you designate a Prey, you know their general location relative to you for the rest of the session, even if they are _Hidden_ or out of sight.

### Mastery Feature

**No Escape**: Once per session, when your Prey attempts to flee a scene by any means, spend 2 Hope. You follow them, appearing in the same location they escape to at the start of the next scene.

## **The Berserker**

_Play the Berserker if you want to trade hits until you're the one still standing and get stronger with every blow you absorb._

### Spellcast Trait

Strength

### Foundation Features

_Pain Is Fuel_: Whenever you mark a Hit Point while you have a Prey designated, gain a +1 bonus to your next damage roll against that Prey.

_Savage Rush_: Once per rest, move to Melee range as part of your attack action without spending a separate move.

### Specialization Feature

**Feed the Frenzy**: Once per scene, after you deal damage to your Prey, mark a Stress to immediately make a second attack against them. This second attack is made with Advantage.

### Mastery Feature

**Titan's Weight**: Once per session, when you reduce your Prey to their last Hit Point, spend 1 Hope. All adversaries within Very Close range who witnessed the attack mark a Stress. Until the end of the session, adversaries will not willingly approach you without first succeeding on a roll to overcome their hesitation.

## Mechanical Notes

You need a Prey designated to be at your best. Make it a priority at the top of every combat encounter. This class rewards aggressive forward movement; standing back or acting defensively works against your strengths. The Houndtooth path is for players who want fights to end through positioning and relentlessness; the Berserker path is for players who want to absorb damage and come out ahead every time. Switching Prey mid-fight is always an option, but losing momentum on a kill costs you the bonus damage buildup.
