### Class Items
_An arcane device of your own construction_ or _a set of annotated technical schematics_
### Hope Feature
_Overclock_: Spend 3 Hope. Both settings of the Apparatus activate simultaneously this scene. The combined effect is determined by your GM based on the nature of your two settings.
### Class Feature
**The Apparatus**
You carry a unique device of your own design with two settings. Define both settings with your GM at the start of play. One should be Study-oriented (analytical, mechanical, detection-based) and one should be Weird-oriented (supernatural, impossible, energy-based). Once per scene, activate one setting. Both settings use your Spellcast Roll when they require active rolls. If destroyed, the Apparatus can be repaired during a long rest.

### Subclasses
As an Æther Engineer, choose between the following subclasses.

**The Mechanist**
_Play the Mechanist if you want the Apparatus to be a precision instrument: reconfigurable, diagnostic, and consistently reliable under pressure._

**The Channeler**
_Play the Channeler if you want to push the Apparatus past its safe limits and see what it can do when the weird starts talking back._
### Background Questions
_Answer any of the following background questions. You can also create your own questions._

- What inspired the original design of the Apparatus? Was it your idea entirely?
- What has the Apparatus done that you didn't intend, and what did you learn from it?
- Who wants the Apparatus, and what have you done to ensure they can't have it?
### Connections
_Ask your fellow players one of the following questions for their characters to answer, or create your own questions._

- You helped me repair the Apparatus after it was damaged. What had gone wrong?
- I used the Apparatus in a way you disapproved of. What did I do?
- You asked me to explain how the Apparatus works. Did my answer satisfy you?
# Subclasses
## **The Mechanist**
_Play the Mechanist if you want the Apparatus to be a precision instrument: reconfigurable, diagnostic, and consistently reliable under pressure._

### Spellcast Trait

Knowledge

### Foundation Features

_Modular Design_: Once per rest, reconfigure one of the Apparatus's two settings. The new setting must remain within the established category (Study-style or Weird-style). The reconfiguration takes a few minutes.

_Diagnostic Mode_: Once per rest, run the Apparatus in a non-active diagnostic mode rather than using a setting. Ask the GM one factual question about the current environment, adversary, or situation. The answer must be truthful.

### Specialization Feature

**Field Modification**: Once per scene, when you use the Apparatus and succeed, add one minor additional effect to the output that you hadn't planned. Work with your GM to keep it consistent with the active setting and reasonably within the device's established capabilities.

### Mastery Feature

**Perfect Calibration**: Once per session, the Apparatus functions flawlessly. Every roll you make using it this session gains +2, and any Critical Success also applies the effect of the second setting for free.
## **The Channeler**
_Play the Channeler if you want to push the Apparatus past its safe limits and see what it can do when the weird starts talking back._

### Spellcast Trait

Knowledge

### Foundation Features

_Push the Limits_: Once per rest, when you use a setting on the Apparatus, push it beyond safe parameters. The effect is enhanced: doubled damage, doubled range, or extended duration, as determined by your GM. After the scene ends, mark 2 Stress as the device requires stabilization.

_Supernatural Feedback_: When supernatural or magical phenomena are nearby, the Apparatus vibrates faintly. You always know if there is active supernatural energy within Close range, without a roll.

### Specialization Feature

**Resonance**: Once per scene, when using the Weird setting of the Apparatus, choose to interact resonantly with any other supernatural effect present in the scene. Make a Spellcast Roll (14). On a success, your effect is amplified (+d6 damage or extended duration). On a failure, the other supernatural effect is neutralized instead.

### Mastery Feature

**Critical Discharge**: Once per session, discharge the full capacity of the Apparatus in a single release. This deals 3d10 magic damage using your Proficiency to all targets within Far range, friend and foe alike. If not used for damage, it creates a massive supernatural phenomenon you and your GM design together. After use, the Apparatus requires a full long rest to recharge.

## Mechanical Notes

Define your Apparatus settings clearly and specifically in your session zero. Each setting should do one thing well, not everything passably. The class is built around the interaction between two focused, distinct powers; vague settings produce vague play. The Mechanist path rewards planning and using the device as reliable infrastructure; the Channeler path rewards aggression and willingness to pay costs for outsized effects. The Hope Feature is your designated big moment, so plan for it and save Hope accordingly.
