_A set of wraps given to you by a mentor_ or _a costume you use for prize fights_

### Hope Feature

_Stick and Move_: Spend 3 Hope and gain a +1 bonus to your Evasion until the end of the scene.

### Class Feature

**Showmanship**
When you make an attack, you can describe the flashy way you engage the adversary. When you do, add your Spellcast Trait instead of your Strength on the attack roll.

# Subclasses

## **Graceful Combatant**

_Play the Graceful Combatant if you want to fluidly move in a way that confounds others and delights observers._

### Spellcast Trait

Agility

### Foundation Features

_Move Like Water_: After you hit an enemy, you can spend a Hope to make an additional move up to Close range away.
_Like a Glove_: Describe how you contort your body to fit into an otherwise inaccessible space. When you do so, mark a Stress and gain access through a normally inaccessible means.

### Specialization Feature

**Raining Blows**: After a successful hit, spend a Hope to make a subsequent attack. This can be repeated as long as your attack is successful.

### Mastery Feature

**Center of the Ring**: Once per scene, when you move after a successful hit, choose one ally within Close range. That ally may also move up to Close range immediately.

## **Brutal Technician**

_Play the Brutal Technician if you want to punish missteps by your opponent in both the battlefield and social gatherings._

### Spellcast Trait

Presence

### Foundation Features

_Powerful Mind, Powerful Body_: When you spend an action studying a target, you gain a +3 bonus to all damage rolls against that target. Similarly, when you roll a successful Instinct roll against a [[Character]], you gain a +3 bonus to Presence rolls concerning that character.
_Snub Nose_: When you fail a roll with Fear, you can mark 2 Stress to change the failure to Hope.

### Specialization Feature

**Speed Bag**: When you roll a second consecutive success with Hope against a target, treat it as a Critical Success and reset the count for this feature.

### Mastery Feature

**Technical Knockdown**: After you trigger **Speed Bag**, the target gains the _Off-Balance_ condition. While _Off-Balance_, their next attack is made with Disadvantage.

## Mechanical Notes

Showmanship is always available. As long as you describe your attack with flair, you're rolling with your strongest trait. The Graceful Combatant path rewards constant movement. Move Like Water generates free positioning, and Raining Blows lets you chain attacks as long as you keep hitting, so don't stand still. The Brutal Technician path rewards focus. Study one target to build your damage bonus, then spend consecutive successful rolls to chain up to Speed Bag's critical trigger. Snub Nose is an insurance policy for when the dice turn against you. Both paths work best when you commit to a primary target early and apply consistent pressure rather than spreading attacks.
