_A collection of rare egg shells_ or _a tool for creating wax impressions_

### Hope Feature

_Close Inspection_: Spend 3 Hope to double your Instinct on your next roll.

### Class Feature

**Finely Tuned Senses**
You are adept at noticing the small details that would escape most. Once per scene, when you make an Instinct roll to investigate or an attack roll, choose one of the following benefits:

- Gain Advantage on the roll.
- Add your Instinct to the result.
- If this is an Instinct roll and you fail with Hope, treat the result as a success with Fear instead.

# Subclasses

## **Carrion Creature**

_Play the Carrion Creature if you want to use strong Instincts to lead to major benefits on successful investigations._

### Spellcast Trait

Instinct

### Foundation Features

_Reading the Room_: You can mark a Stress and make an Instinct roll against a [[Character]]. On a success, you learn the [[Attitude]] of a character towards you or another member of your party.

_Detail-oriented_: On a successful Instinct roll to investigate, gain one of these benefits immediately:

- Gain a Hope. This is in addition to any Hope gained normally via your roll.
- Clear a Stress.
- Find a mundane item of your choosing.

### Specialization Feature

**Make the Most of it**: On a failure with Fear, you can spend a Hope to give all other party members a Hope.

### Mastery Feature

**Relentless Scrounger**: Once per scene, after you use _Finely Tuned Senses_, you can immediately move to Close range. If the triggering roll succeeds, clear 1 Stress.

## **Needler**

_Play the Needler if you want to make highly opportunistic moves to take down much stronger foes._

### Spellcast Trait

Instinct

### Foundation Features

_Weak Spots_: Make an Instinct Action Roll against an adversary. On a success, you deal double damage to that adversary until the end of the scene.
_Quick Snap_: When you take damage from an adversary, you can spend a Hope to gain Advantage on all rolls against that adversary.

### Specialization Feature

**Vicious strike**: On a successful attack, you may mark a Hit Point to give the target the _Exposed_ condition. Treat the next successful attack against an _Exposed_ creature as a critical success no matter the outcome.

### Mastery Feature

**Needlepoint Finish**: When you hit a target with _Exposed_, gain 1 Hope and clear 1 Stress. If this attack downs the target, you may immediately mark another target within Close range as _Exposed_.

## Mechanical Notes

Finely Tuned Senses is flexible so read the situation before you decide which option to take. Advantage is safest. Adding your Instinct is strongest when your score is high. The failure-to-success flip is most valuable to steer outcomes when you need a success. The Carrion Creature path converts every investigation into a resource. Look for scenes where you can stack Hope, Stress clears, and items from a single roll sequence. Make the Most of it turns bad group luck into a recovery moment; use it before your party fully processes a fear-failure. The Needler path is pure burst: double damage from one Instinct strike, then build the Exposed chain to maximize a single target. Both paths benefit from using your class feature every scene.
