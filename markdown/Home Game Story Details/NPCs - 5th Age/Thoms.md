Thoms is an ordinate [Riolan](Riolan.md), bred specifically for management of large organizations. As such, he focuses primarily on the day-to-day operations of his family's business, but if pressed will access the shared knowledge of his kindred to interact on larger topics.|

|Appearance:|Short and fat for a Riolan, Thoms' skin is notably faded compared to some of the more vibrant Riolans in the city. They do nothing to adorn themselves, making only a passing show of applying the body paints that are in fashion amongst their kind. They wear a black fur on their back with several pale gray spikes of about six inches long protruding from it. These spikes rattle when Thoms moves.|
|Personality:|Business-like and generally uninterested in things that do not have to do with logistics or the moving of goods. Will make polite conversation, and sees people as part of a larger system designed to move goods from one part of Tidwell to another.|
