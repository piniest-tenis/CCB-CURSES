A chapterhouse head of the [[Church of the Bent Trunk]] in the heart of Reveille. Gregarious, but sly.
 
Species: [Hokaw](Hokaw.md)
 
Appearance: Black plumage that is disheveled and missing several feathers. One white eye, obviously blind. Missing his right hand.
 
Personality: Outgoing, charming, and kind. Genuinely cares for the people he oversees. Believe wholeheartedly in the Church but questions Gwennet.
 
Plot hook: has a cursed compass he was given by a member of the copse, which he took willingly knowing it would cause him to become unretrievably lost whenever traveling somewhere unfamiliar. There's a sick gecko-person who needs a medicine available in the bazaar, but he cannot get it himself.
