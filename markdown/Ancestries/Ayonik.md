A group of stout land dwellers with heavy, oiled coats and large glassy eyes. Loquacious and considered extremely good neighbors, the Ayonik are rarely found away from the [[Allonaut]]s they live in harmony with on the shores of the [[Erelbaad Lake]] in [[Taliga]].

Ayonik bear great resemblance to fat otters, and their cherubic faces and round black eyes are often co-opted by merchants on labels and signage as a short-hand for trustworthiness. However, as anyone who has directly dealt with an enraged Ayonik knows, they are fully capable of extreme violence in defense of their close compatriots and ideals.
