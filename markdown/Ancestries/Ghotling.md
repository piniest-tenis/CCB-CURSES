Medium-sized species that bears a distinctive hunch to their postures and inordinately long arms. Their pallid grey skin and warty features often cause them to be viewed as nuisances or bad luck, but they are an exceedingly cunning people able to ingratiate themselves to prominent figures within systems of power. 

Their skills as spies, managers and engineers have them often found in places of high employ, though they can just as often be found in criminal gangs or as the heads of vast networks of confidence artists.

# Ancestry

**Long Arms**: Treat any weapon, ability, spell or other feature that has a Melee range as though it has a Very Close range instead.

**Shrewd**: Spend a Hope to gain advantage on an Instinct trait roll.
