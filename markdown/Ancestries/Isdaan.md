A race of fish-like characters that live a semi-aquatic life. Primarily from the [Medilaac](The%20Land%20of%20Tidwell/World%20Details/Nations%20&%20States/Carnithia/Medilaac/Medilaac.md) region, they can be found in almost every city in Tidwell due to their ability to broadly adapt their custom to the societies around them. 

Isdaan are often agrarian when in their own communities. They settle on the banks of streams, rivers, lakes and the sea where they build kelp farms and their nests for spawning. Intensely family-oriented.

Bipedal, with long flat feet and webbed toes, as well as a set of exposed gills at the edge of their jaws, giving them an almost bearded appearance. Thin bodies, often top-heavy, causing them to have a hunched appearance on land. Very graceful and fluid in the water, however.

# Ancestry

**Amphibious**: You can breathe and move naturally underwater.

**Socially Practiced**: When rolling to influence a Character, roll an additional d6. If you roll a 6, add a +5 modifier to your roll and then roll again.
