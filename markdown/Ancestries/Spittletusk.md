A scaled creature with long necks and slender, needle like tusks that jut from their lower jaws. Large, ridged eyebrows, and thin, delicate limbs. Extremely dexterous and flexible.

This species originally referred to themselves as Shiandrans, but have proudly adopted the pejorative term Spittletusk given to them by less generous societies when they were a new species on Tidwell. Their societies value cunning and depth of consideration more than most, and venerate a nameless trickster god whose mark is two crossed tusks.

Intensely strategic, Spittletusk find great success historically in societies structured around trade, where they are known as shrewd and calculating negotiators.

# Ancestry

**Dexterous**: When you make an Agility roll, you can mark a Stress to gain advantage on that roll.

**Venomous**: When you succeed on an attack against a target within Melee range, you can spend a Hope to inject a paralytic nerve agent via your tusks, causing the target to become _Restrained_ until the end of the Scene. _Restrained_ characters cannot move unless they succeed a Strength Roll (13) or mark a Stress.
