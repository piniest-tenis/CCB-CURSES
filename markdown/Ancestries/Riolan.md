A species of characters bearing a deep violet skin, and a partial hard carapace of segmented black keratin covering their heads, necks and bellies.

They have six sets of thin armlike appendages with small grasping claws at their ends, and another six sets of legs that are short and stiff, leaving their bellies to rub on the ground while they move.

They wear ornate costumes that mostly cover their upper limbs and backs, often consisting of the hides of predators found throughout Tidwell.

Riolans exchange a base cultural and personal understanding of each other through exchange of cud, which is performed at nearly every meeting between two or more Riolans. This cud contains a microbe load that serves as conveyance of the larger opinions and thoughts of the Riolan species.

Cave Riolans tend to be larger and paler than their City Riolan cousins.

# Ancestry

**Cud-munication**: When interacting with another Riolan within Close range, you may make a Presence Roll (12). On a success, you may convey specific information that will propagate to all other characters within Riolan ancestry over a period of a few days.

**Communal**: When you take a downtime action to Tend to Wounds or Tend to All Wounds, you can choose an additional target.
