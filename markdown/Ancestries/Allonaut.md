A hard-shelled aquatic race of creatures with wildly varied physiology between members. The Allonauts are intelligent and fiercely loyal, often forming lifelong bonds with [[Ayonik]] partners.

Allonauts are a little known species outside the [[The Land of Tidwell/World Details/Nations & States/The Waste/Varjalune Republic/Varjalune Republic]], as they rarely venture far from their natural habitats. Rarely, an Allonaut will make a name for themselves, but given the vast diversity of species that rise and fall within Tidwell most assume it them to be one of the brief flashes of sentience that emerge amongst all kinds of creatures on the continent.

# Ancestry

**Hard Carapace:** Gain a +2 bonus to your armor score

**Fiercely Loyal**: Mark a Stress to cause an attack against another player to be rolled with Disadvantage
