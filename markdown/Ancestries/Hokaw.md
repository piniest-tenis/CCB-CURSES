Birdlike humanoids, crested heads, long arms and short legs, beady eyes that are usually black. Hokaw inhabit an inordinate amount of positions in leadership throughout Tidwell. Their commanding voices and ease of communication naturally lends the Hokaw an air of authority.

Their culture is one that glorifies suicidal altruism. Hokaw culture is replete with stories of heroes (sometimes wantonly) sacrificing themselves for the better of all people. As such, Hokaw routinely find themselves as religious leaders and soldiers.

# Ancestry

**Eagle-Eyed**: You have Advantage on Instinct trait rolls related to sight.

**Commanding Voice**: Once per session, you can mark a Stress to reroll a Presence trait roll with Advantage.
