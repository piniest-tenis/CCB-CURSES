Lothrians are one of the rarer of the common species of Tidwell, and are often regarded with some awe on accordance of that fact. Most people within Tidwell will go their entire lives without ever encountering one.

Lothrians are small, jewel-eyed relatives of mollusks, and bear an adaptable body template. A typical Lothrian stands less than a meter at its tallest, and due to their flexible body plan often resemble the societies in which they choose to inhabit.

Lothrians can come both in pygmy and mammoth, with the latter rivaling a [[Human]]'s stature, and the former coming in at no larger than a bread box.

# Ancestry

**Camouflage**: Gain advantage on rolls to Hide.

**Lucky**: When you spend a Hope, roll a six-sided die. On a 6, you gain a Hope and re-roll this die.
