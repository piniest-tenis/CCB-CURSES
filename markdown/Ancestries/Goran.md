One of the native species of Tidwell. Apparently composed of nothing but rocks held together with a fleshy moss-like substance. Little is known about the society or culture of these people, as they are intensely cloistered, but occasionally a foundling will be discovered abandoned on trips to [Rhodia](Rhodia.md). 

When raised in society with other species, Gorans are capable of speech but in a labored and slow manner as their anatomy is obviously unsuited for it normally.

# Ancestry

**Hardened**: Gain +1 bonus to your Armor score and an additional Hit Point at character creation.

**Adaptable Physiology**: You can cause yourself to disassemble into a pile of large rocks. You can use this in combat to make an attack on every target within melee range, treating it as a Strength weapon that deals d8 physical damage using your Proficiency.
