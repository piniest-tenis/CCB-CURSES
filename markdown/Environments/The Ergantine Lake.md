*The crystal clear waters of Ergantine Lake stretch out before you, deceptively calm. Beneath the surface, massive shapes drift in the deep — creatures transformed by decades of proximity to Forestdown's corruption. Some are benign, even beautiful in their strangeness. Others are not. The lake is the lifeblood of Gatehouse, providing fish, trade routes, and drinking water, but it demands respect. The old Tenders say you can tell how deep the Creep has gotten by how strange the fish are running. Lately, the fish have been running very strange indeed.*
**Tone**: Eerie, wondrous, treacherous
**Tier 1**
**Type**: Exploration
**Difficulty**: 12

**Potential Adversaries**:
- [[Skelkandi]]
- [[Quaddadura]]
- [[Creep-Touched Deaconfish]]
- [[Glowfry School]]

**Features**
**Crystal Depths - Passive**
The water of Ergantine Lake is unnaturally clear. PCs can see deep beneath the surface, granting Advantage on Instinct rolls to spot submerged creatures or objects within Close range. However, this clarity works both ways — submerged creatures can see the PCs just as easily.
_"You can see all the way to the bottom. Every stone, every weed, every shadow. That's the problem. The shadows can see you too."_

**Creep Currents - Passive**
Dark tendrils of Creep drift through the water in unpredictable currents. A PC who enters the water must make an Agility Reaction Roll (11) each time they take an action while submerged or mark a Stress from contact with the corruption. A PC who marks 2 Stress this way gains the _Creep-Touched_ condition.
_"The water itself feels wrong against your skin — not cold exactly, but aware."_

**Something Stirs Below - Active**
Spend a Fear. A massive shape passes beneath the party's position — a shadow that blots out the lakebed for a long, terrible moment before moving on. All PCs must make a Presence Reaction Roll (12) or become Frightened, gaining Disadvantage on their next action roll. If the party is on the water, their vessel rocks violently.
_"For a moment the light changes. Something vast moves beneath you, and then it is gone, and the water is still again, and no one speaks."_

**Lakebed Discovery - Active**
A PC who investigates the lakebed (Knowledge or Instinct Roll, 12) discovers something of interest — a sunken Teetle Cart with its cargo intact, an ancient Sallidean Stone carving half-buried in silt, or the remains of a previous expedition. The discovery provides either useful equipment or a clue about the lake's deeper mysteries.
_"Beneath the sediment, your fingers close around something smooth and carved. It is older than the Keep."_
