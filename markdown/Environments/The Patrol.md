*The edge of Forestdown is not a line on a map. It is a feeling — the point where the air changes, where the birdsong stops, where the undergrowth takes on that dark, fibrous quality that the Tenders know means the forest is pushing outward. Your patrol route runs along this edge, a well-salted path that has held the boundary for decades. Today, the salt line is gone. In its place, a wall of new growth has erupted overnight — thick, dark, and spreading visibly as you watch. The Creep is blooming.*
**Tone**: Urgent, overwhelming, unprecedented
**Tier 1**
**Type**: Event
**Difficulty**: 12

**Potential Adversaries**:
- [[Bloom Tendrils]]
- [[Bloom Heart]]
- [[Madanikuputukas (Swarm)]]
- [[Creep-Bound Etherotaxic Entity 1]]

**Features**
**Spreading Corruption - Passive**
The Creep Bloom expands the boundary of Forestdown in real time. This is tracked as a Progress Countdown (8). Each time the GM spends a Fear or a GM turn passes without the PCs making progress toward stopping the Bloom, mark a segment. When the countdown completes, the Bloom has expanded far enough to threaten Gatehouse's outer settlements and the scene shifts from containment to retreat.
_"You can see it moving. The dark tendrils creep across the salted earth as though the salt were nothing. This has never happened before."_

**Toxic Ground - Passive**
The newly Bloomed ground is saturated with concentrated Creep. A PC who moves through Bloomed terrain takes 1d4 magical damage. A PC who ends their turn standing on Bloomed terrain must make an Agility Reaction Roll (11) or mark a Stress. This damage and Stress cannot be avoided by any means other than leaving the Bloomed area.
_"The ground is soft underfoot and it clings to your boots. Where you step, small things wriggle in the disturbed soil."_

**Call for Backup - Active**
A PC can attempt to send a runner to Gatehouse for reinforcements — a Medicament burn team. This requires a Presence Roll (12) to convince a fellow Tender to leave the group, or the PC can go themselves. If successful, reinforcements arrive when the Spreading Corruption countdown reaches 6, turning the tide but not before the damage is done.
_"Someone has to go. Someone has to tell them. But that means leaving the rest of us here with... this."_

**Creep Bloom Surge - Active**
Spend 2 Fear. The Bloom accelerates violently. The ground heaves, new tendrils erupt in a wave, and a burst of spores fills the air. All PCs must make an Agility Reaction Roll (12) or be knocked prone and take 1d8+2 magical damage. Mark two segments on the Spreading Corruption countdown.
_"The earth splits open and the forest roars forward. It is not an attack. It is not malice. It is growth — mindless, unstoppable, hungry growth."_
