*A reinforced chamber deep within the destroyed northern tower of Eskikale Keep, rebuilt by the Etherements for a singular purpose: the containment and study of captured Etherotaxia. Iron-banded doors seal from the outside. Sallidean Stone wards line the walls, their carvings old and of uncertain origin. The Etherements use this space to observe how greater Etherotaxia behave under confinement — how they move, react, and eventually expire. Today, the party has been locked inside with one such specimen, either as a test of their capabilities or because Rearcaptain Oramora wanted to see what would happen.*
**Tone**: Claustrophobic, tense, morally ambiguous
**Tier 1**
**Type**: Event
**Difficulty**: 12

**Potential Adversaries**:
- [[Captured Etherotaxic Entity]]

**Features**
**Sealed Chamber - Passive**
The iron-banded doors are sealed from the outside and cannot be forced open by conventional means. The Sallidean Stone wards prevent the Etherotaxic entity from passing through the walls. Breaking the wards requires a Knowledge or Finesse Roll (14) and renders the containment permanently useless — the Etherements will not be pleased.
_"The door shuts behind you with a sound like a coffin lid. Through the observation slit, you see Oramora's single visible eye watching."_

**Observation - Passive**
The Etherements are watching through observation slits in the walls. Any PC action is being cataloged. If the PCs resolve the encounter socially rather than through combat, the Etherements' reaction is one of disturbed fascination rather than approval — this was not the expected outcome.
_"You feel the weight of eyes on you from every angle. They are not watching to help. They are watching to learn."_

**Escalating Containment - Active**
Spend a Fear. The Etherements activate one of the Sallidean Stone wards from outside, causing it to pulse with energy. All creatures in the chamber take 1d6+1 magical damage. The Etherements use this to "encourage" the entity to be more aggressive if the encounter is proceeding too calmly.
_"The wards flare. The entity screams — not with sound, but with something you feel behind your eyes. Oramora's voice comes through the slit: 'Interesting. Again.'"_

**The Choice - Active**
Spend a Fear. A PC notices a mechanism near the door — a release lever, rusted but functional, that can be reached from inside. Using it opens the chamber. The Etherements will attempt to re-seal the door (Agility Reaction Roll 12 to hold it open), and the consequences of releasing the entity into the Keep will unfold from there.
_"There. Behind the grime on the wall. A handle. They built a way out, once. Before Oramora decided the experiments were more important than safety protocols."_
