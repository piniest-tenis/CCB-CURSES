Goblins are small humanoids easily recognizable by their large eyes and massive membranous ears. With keen hearing and sharp eyesight, they perceive details both at great distances and in darkness, allowing them to move through less-optimal environments with ease.

**Surefooted**: You ignore disadvantage on Agility Rolls.

**Danger Sense**: Once per rest, mark a Stress to force an adversary to reroll an attack against you or an ally within Very Close range.
