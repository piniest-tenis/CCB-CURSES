Simiah resemble anthropomorphic monkeys and apes with long limbs and prehensile feet. They are skilled climbers who can easily transition from bipedal movement to knuckle-walking and climbing, and back again.

**Natural Climber**: You have advantage on Agility Rolls that involve balancing and climbing.

**Nimble**: Gain a permanent +1 bonus to your Evasion at character creation.
