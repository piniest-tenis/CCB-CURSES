Halflings are small humanoids with large hairy feet and prominent rounded ears. They are naturally attuned to the magnetic fields of the Mortal Realm, granting them a strong internal compass and acute senses of hearing and smell.

**Luckbringer**: At the start of each session, everyone in your party gains a Hope.

**Internal Compass**: When you roll a 1 on your Hope Die, you can reroll it.
