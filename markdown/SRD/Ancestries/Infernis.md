Infernis are humanoids who possess sharp canine teeth, pointed ears, and horns. They are the descendants of demons from the Circles Below and possess a dread visage that manifests both involuntarily and purposefully.

**Fearless**: When you roll with Fear, you can mark 2 Stress to change it into a roll with Hope instead.

**Dread Visage**: You have advantage on rolls to intimidate hostile creatures.
