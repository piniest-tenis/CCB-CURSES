Galapa resemble anthropomorphic turtles with large, domed shells into which they can retract. Members of this ancestry can draw their head, arms, and legs into their shell for protection to use it as a natural shield when defensive measures are needed.

**Shell**: Gain a bonus to your damage thresholds equal to your Proficiency.

**Retract**: Mark a Stress to retract into your shell. While in your shell, you have resistance to physical damage, you have disadvantage on action rolls, and you can't move.
