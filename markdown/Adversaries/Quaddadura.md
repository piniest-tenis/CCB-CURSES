**Tier 1 Bruiser**
_A massive, furred, thick-skinned semi-aquatic creature ranging from ten to thirty feet long. Large spade-like teeth surround four prominent hook-shaped eye teeth. Normally a deep-water feeder on lungfish, prolonged Creep exposure has driven some Quaddadura into the shallows where they are far more aggressive than their uncorrupted kin. The fur along their backs has taken on a dark, matted quality, and their eyes glow faintly in low light._
**Motives & Tactics**: Feed, defend territory, surface aggression
***
Difficulty: 11
Thresholds: 7/13
HP: 7
Stress: 2
Attack: +0
Weapon: Bite: Melee | 1d10+2 Physical
		Ram: Close | 1d8+1 Physical
***
**Features**
**Slow**: When you spotlight this adversary and they don't have a token on their stat block, they can't act yet. Place a token on their stat block. When they have a token, clear it and they can act.
**Thrash**: On a successful Bite attack, the Quaddadura shakes its target violently. The target is moved to Melee range if not already and is knocked prone.
**Territorial Bellow - Reaction**: When a PC moves within Close range of the Quaddadura for the first time, it lets out a deafening bellow. The PC must make a Presence Reaction Roll (11) or become Frightened, gaining Disadvantage on attacks against the Quaddadura until the end of their next turn.
