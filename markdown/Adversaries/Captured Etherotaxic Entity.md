**Tier 1 Solo**
_A towering, semi-corporeal figure that shifts between forms — now a column of pale light, now a shape that almost resembles something familiar, now a tangle of limbs that reach toward you with what might be curiosity or might be hunger. Unlike the smaller Etherotaxia bound to the Creep, this entity radiates an intelligence that is unmistakable. Its movements are deliberate. It watches. It waits. When it reaches toward you, it is not attacking. It is trying to communicate. The Etherements do not know this, or do not care._
**Motives & Tactics**: Communicate, escape captivity, understand
***
Difficulty: 12
Thresholds: 10/16
HP: 8
Stress: 4
Attack: +2
Weapon: Desperate Lash: Close | 1d8+2 Magical
***
Experience: Whispers of Ichida +3
***
**Features**
**Semi-Corporeal**: This entity takes half damage from physical attacks.
**Relentless (2)**: This adversary can be spotlighted up to 2 times per GM turn. Spend Fear as usual to spotlight them.
**Resonance - Passive**: PCs within Close range hear fragments of thought — not words, but impressions of emotion, memory, and intent. A PC who succeeds on a Presence or Instinct Roll (12) understands that the entity is frightened and wants to leave, not fight.
**Plaintive Reach - Active**: The entity extends a limb toward a PC. This is not an attack. The PC must make a Presence Reaction Roll (11). On a failure, they flinch and the entity recoils, marking a Stress. On a success, the PC receives a vivid flash of memory — the entity being captured, dragged into the Keep, confined. This grants the PC Advantage on all subsequent Presence rolls to communicate with the entity.
**Cornered Fury - Reaction**: When the entity has marked half or more of its HP, its attacks deal an additional 1d6 magical damage and it no longer attempts to communicate. If a PC has previously succeeded on Resonance or Plaintive Reach, they can spend their turn making a Presence Roll (13) to calm the entity and end Cornered Fury.
**Release - Active**: If the PCs open the chamber door or otherwise create an escape route while the entity is not in Cornered Fury, it departs peacefully. All PCs gain a Hope. The entity pauses at the threshold and projects a final impression — gratitude, and a warning about something deeper in the forest.
