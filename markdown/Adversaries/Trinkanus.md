**Tier 1 Solo**
_A large, flat-headed cave creature. Possesses two long, thin, whip-like forelimbs segmented at its front. It has a broad, saw-like mandible and several dull crusty eyes. It is of an order of creatures that survive by "ranching," they mind and control populations of another population of creatures carefully, picking off only those that it needs to sustain itself, otherwise providing crude subsistence means for those creatures to survive._
**Motives & Tactics**: Eat, Farm, Survive
***
Difficulty: 12
Thresholds: 10/16
HP: 9
Stress: 3
Attack: +2
Weapon: Pincer: Close | 1d6+1 Physical
		Bite: Melee | 2d8 Physical
***
**Features**
**Grab**: On a successful attack with a Pincer, the Trinkanus can move its target up to Close range.
**Rancher - Passive**: The Trinkanus will not attack creatures it sees as part of its "herd." A creature moved by _Grab_ will not attack unless it's moved.
**Consume**: On a successful attack with a Bite, the Trinkanus can attempt to swallow the target. The target must make a Strength Roll (11) or be swallowed whole. A target who has been swallowed whole cannot act until they are free from the stomach of the Trinkanus.
