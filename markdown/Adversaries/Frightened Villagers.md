**Tier 2 Social**
_Panicked villagers desperately trying to escape, but too scared to sensibly follow directions_
**Motives & Tactics**: Escape, survive, save possessions
***
Difficulty: 13
Thresholds: 6/13
HP: 3
Stress: 4
Attack: -1
Weapon: Trample: Melee | d8+2 Physical
***
**Features**
**Escalating Fear**: Whenever you gain Fear during this encounter, the Frightened Villagers deal an additional d8 damage with their Trample.
**Unruly Mob**: If the Frightened Villagers move through Melee range of a PC, the PC automatically takes damage from their Trample weapon.
**Unreasoning**: If the PCs fail a social interaction to calm the mob, they must mark a Stress. When the mob has caused 3 stress to be marked this way, they must **Flee**.
**Flee**: The Frightened Villagers moves as a group away from the most visible threat at maximum speed.
