**Tier 1 Leader**
_At the heart of the Bloom, something pulses. It is not a creature — or if it is, it bears no resemblance to any cataloged Etherotaxia. A mass of dense, knotted Creep roughly the size of a wagon, shot through with veins of pale bioluminescence. The tendrils radiate from it. The Madanikuputukas swarm around it protectively. It grows visibly, moment by moment, and with each pulse the Forestdown border lurches another foot outward. The Tenders have never seen anything like this. No one has._
**Motives & Tactics**: Expand Forestdown, defend the Bloom, summon protectors
***
Difficulty: 12
Thresholds: 7/12
HP: 6
Stress: 3
Attack: +0
Weapon: Creep Pulse: Close | 1d8+2 Magical
***
**Features**
**Rooted - Passive**: The Bloom Heart cannot move. It is anchored to the ground. However, it does not need to be spotlighted to use its Reaction features.
**Bloom Expansion - Passive**: At the end of each GM turn, the area of Creep expands. Any PC who was previously outside the Creep's area and is now within it must make an Agility Reaction Roll (11) or mark a Stress from sudden contact with the corruption.
**Call the Swarm - Active**: Spend a Fear. A new [[Madanikuputukas (Swarm)]] emerges from the Bloom Heart and is immediately spotlighted.
**Creep Surge - Reaction**: When the Bloom Heart takes damage, it releases a wave of Creep energy. All creatures within Close range must make an Agility Reaction Roll (12) or take 1d4 magical damage.
**Vulnerability - Passive**: The Bloom Heart takes double damage from fire. If it is destroyed, all Bloom Tendrils in the scene lose their Regenerate feature and the Bloom's expansion halts immediately.
