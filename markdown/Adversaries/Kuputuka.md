**Tier 2 Solo**
_A massive, low slung, elongated eight-legged creature. It is semi-corporeal, and considerably more intelligent than its progenitors._
**Motives & Tactics**: Defend, escape, spread creep
***
Difficulty: 15
Thresholds: 13/25
HP: 9
Stress: 3
Attack: +4
Weapon: Crush: Melee | 2d20+3 Magical
***
**Features**
**Semi-Corporeal**: The Kuputuka halves incoming physical damage.
**Force Transform**: Spend a Fear to force a target that is _Creep-Touched_ to develop an abnormal transformation. The target takes 2d6+3 magical damage.
**Disassociation Aura**: Creatures within Close Range must succeed at an Instinct Roll (13) in order to target this creature with an attack.
**Compounding Dread**: When the Kuputuka successfully attacks a player character, gain a Fear.
