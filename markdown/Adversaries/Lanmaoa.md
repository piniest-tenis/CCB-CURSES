**Tier 1 Standard**
_A race of forest dwellers. Pint sized. Known to crawl under doors and through small cracks. Little is known of these, and their appearance is often linked to the early stages of delusions in those affected by the [[Creep]]. This may be due to the intense psychotropic effect on those who spend any amount of time in their presence._
**Motives & Tactics**: Move, Evade, Steal
***
Difficulty: 14
Thresholds: 4/8
HP: 2
Stress: 4
Attack: -1
Weapon: Bite: Melee | 1d4-1 Physical
***
**Features**
**Confusion - Passive**: Any target within Close range of a Lanamoa must make a Presence Roll (14) or become _Confused_. A _Confused_ creature gains Disadvantage on Presence, Instinct and Knowledge trait rolls.
**Hallucination**: Spend a Fear to create a shared hallucination for all targets within Close range of the Lanamoa. A character can dismiss this effect by succeeding in a Presence, Instinct or Knowledge roll against the Lanamoa's difficulty.
**Thieve**: Once per scene, a Lanamoa can steal an item from a _Confused_ target, or a target which is under the effect of a Hallucination.
