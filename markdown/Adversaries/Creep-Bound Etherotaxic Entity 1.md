**Tier 1 Standard**
_Any of the numerous body plans of the etherotaxic entities that live in Forestdown. They follow variable body plans and motivations, but like all etherotaxia their motives can be unique to the individual._
**Motives & Tactics**: alter, advance, establish new territory
***
Difficulty: 12
Thresholds: 5/9
HP: 3
Stress: 3
Attack: +1
Weapon: Touch | 3d4 Magical
***
Experience: Whispers of Ichida +2
***
**Features**
**Semi-Corporeal**: Etherotaxic characters take half damage from physical attacks.
**Flickering Displace**: Spend a Fear to move this creature up to Far distance instantly. 
**Creep**: All targets within melee range must succeed against an Agility reaction roll or mark a Stress. Any character that marks two Stress this way gains the _Creep-Touched_ condition. A _Creep-Touched_ creature has disadvantage on all action rolls until the condition is cleared narratively.
