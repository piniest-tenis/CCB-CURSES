**Tier 1 Horde**
_Thick, ropy tendrils of living Creep that erupt from the ground during a Bloom event. They are not creatures in any conventional sense — they are the forest itself, extending its reach with terrifying speed. Each tendril is as thick as a human arm, dark and fibrous, and moves with a blind grasping urgency. Where they take root, the ground darkens and new undergrowth begins to spread within minutes. Cutting them only slows the advance; the stumps regenerate unless the root mass is destroyed or burned._
**Motives & Tactics**: Spread, root, entangle
***
Difficulty: 10
Thresholds: 4/8
HP: 5
Stress: 1
Attack: -1
Weapon: Lash: Melee | 1d6+1 Physical
***
**Features**
**Horde**: When the Bloom Tendrils have marked half or more of their HP, their Lash deals 2 damage instead.
**Entangle - Reaction**: When a PC moves within Melee range of the Bloom Tendrils, the tendrils attempt to grapple them. The PC must make an Agility Reaction Roll (11) or become Restrained. A Restrained PC cannot move and has Disadvantage on attack rolls until they break free with a Strength Roll (11) on their turn.
**Regenerate - Passive**: At the start of each GM turn, if the Bloom Tendrils have not taken fire damage since the last GM turn, clear 1 HP from their track. Fire damage prevents this regeneration for one full round.
