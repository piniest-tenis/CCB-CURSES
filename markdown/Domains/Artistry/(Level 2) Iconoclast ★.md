After a long rest, gain a number of tokens equal to your Proficiency. When you interact with a [[Character]] which has a negative [[Attitude]] towards you, you can spend any number of tokens on this card. For every token spent, gain one of the following effects:
- Add +1 to the result of a roll involving the character
- Spend a Hope to cause the target of your interaction to mark a Stress
Clear unused tokens at the start of a long rest.
***
**Curse**: If you have a negative [[Reputation]] with a [[Faction]], members of that faction have a -1 penalty to their [[Attitude]] towards you.
