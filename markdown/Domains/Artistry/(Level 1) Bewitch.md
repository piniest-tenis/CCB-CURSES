You may use a piece of your art to influence a [[Character]] or [[Creature]]. Make a Spellcast Roll against a target within Close range. If you succeed, the affected target becomes _Ensorcelled_. The Ensorcelled target is affected for the duration of its next turn. Choose from one of the following commands to issue to the target:
- Stop - The affected target stops its current activity and freezes in place, taking no action
- Retreat - The affected target moves as far away from you as it can
- Come - The affected target approaches you until it is in Very Close range
