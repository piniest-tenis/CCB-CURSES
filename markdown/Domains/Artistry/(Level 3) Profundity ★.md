Make a Spellcast Roll against all [[Character|Characters]] which can hear you. On a success, choose from one of the following effects:
- _Convince_: For the duration of the scene, affected characters gain a +1 bonus to [[Attitude]] towards you. 
- _Confuse_: Temporarily _Stun_ affected targets, causing them to mark a Stress. _Stunned_ characters cannot use reactions and cannot take other actions until this condition is cleared. This can affect allies.
- _Castigate_: Temporarily _Shame_ affected targets, causing them to mark a Stress. _Shamed_ characters gain a -2 penalty to their Difficulty.
***
**Curse**: Your newfound depth makes connecting with others a chore. When you use the abilities on this Domain Card, place a token on it. When you take a long rest before any downtime activities, mark a Stress for every token on this card. After the rest, clear all tokens.
