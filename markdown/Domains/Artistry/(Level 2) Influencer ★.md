After a long rest, add a number of tokens to this card equal to your Presence. When you make a roll to influence a [[Character]] which has a positive [[Attitude]] towards you, you can spend a token from this card to add +2 to the result of your roll.
Clear unspent tokens at the start of a long rest.

**Curse**: After a long rest, choose an ally. That ally marks a Stress.
