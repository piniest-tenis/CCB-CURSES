You gain a special, one-time downtime activity called _Masterwork_ with a Countdown Tracker of 5. While this project is underway, you gain a +1 bonus to your Presence, Instinct, or Knowledge. Once complete, select one of the following permanent benefits:
- _Hearts and Minds_: Place a number of tokens on this card equal to your Tier. You can spend these tokens to gain a +1 bonus to [[Reputation]] with any [[Faction]] whose current score is -1 or greater.
- _Authority_: Choose Presence, Instinct, or Knowledge. You gain a +2 bonus to the chosen trait, and allies gain a +1.
- _Screed_: Choose a Faction. That Faction takes a –1 Reputation penalty with every Faction whose current Reputation with them is +0 or higher.

This domain card does not need to remain in your loadout for these benefits to persist.
