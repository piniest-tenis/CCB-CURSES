You become exceptionally gifted in performance, and have the ability to command a crowd's attention. You gain an additional short rest downtime action called "Workshopping." Every time you engage in this action, put three tokens on this card. You can spend one of these tokens to gain any of the following effects:
- Add your Proficiency bonus to a Presence roll
- A [[Character]] treats you with a +1 bonus to [[Attitude]]
- Within the same scene, re-use the result of a Presence roll
***
**Curse**: Your sensibilities sometimes veer towards the "avant garde." Whenever you fail a Presence roll with Fear, the GM may introduce an unintended consequence. If you have the [[(Level 3) Prophet ★ ↔]] Domain Card in your loadout, you may spend a Hope to force this to be a positive effect.
