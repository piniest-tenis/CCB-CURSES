Once per scene, when you roll with Hope, you may "save" the result of your Duality Dice. When you do so, add a number of tokens to this card equal to the value of your Hope die, and then re-roll your Hope die. Those tokens can be spent to add a +1 modifier to subsequent rolls.

At the end of the scene, clear all unused tokens.

**Curse**: Your extrasensory perception has come at cost to your regular senses. Any roll which involves using one of your senses directly gains Disadvantage. If you have the [[(Level 3) Sharpshooter ★ ↔]] Domain Card in your loadout, ignore the Hidden condition when making attacks or Spellcast Rolls against your target.
