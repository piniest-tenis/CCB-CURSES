You gain the ability to cast supernatural spells. This spellcasting comes from your ability to deceive reality into believing it is something that it is not. If you have the [[(Level 7) Weird-Touched]] domain card in your loadout, when you use your magic against a [[Character]] or [[Creature]] that would not cause damage, gain Advantage on any Spellcast Roll.

Gain a number of tokens equal to your Level. You may spend these tokens to cast spells. These spells are of your own creation and not limited by existing spells on other domains. Work with your GM by describing the spell so that they can set an appropriate Difficulty to create the spell. You may spend additional tokens to reduce the Difficulty by one per token spent.

---

**Curse**: Your magic isn't magic at all, but rather unmaking and remaking the reality of Tidwell to suit your purposes. This has angered one of the deities of Tidwell, and they and their devotees have a vendetta against you. If you have the [[(Level 3) Mugger ★ ↔]] Domain Card, you have instead stolen this ability from the deity, and a sect of adherents of that faith now worship you instead.
