Talents inherent in you since birth are awakening. You gain the ability to cast supernatural spells.

These spells are of your own creation and description. Work with your GM by describing the spell so that they can set an appropriate Difficulty to create the spell.

After a long rest, place a number of tokens on this card equal to your Level. After you cast a spell, you can spend tokens to recall a previously crafted spell, or to add one of these effects to it.

- **Destructive**: Deal d8 damage to the target using your Proficiency. At Tier 3, this increases to d10. At Tier 4, this increases to d12.
- **Deafening**: [[Character|Characters]] and [[Creature|Creatures]] within Close range of the target cannot hear until the end of the Scene. 
- **Blinding**: Characters and Creatures within Close range of the target cannot see until the end of the Scene.
- **Opportune**: Characters and Creatures affected by this spell choose to clear one Stress or one Hit Point.
---
**Curse**: Your spellcasting is more an "art" than a science. Whenever you use your spellcasting, roll a d4. If the result is 1, the GM describes an unintended consequence of their choosing. If you have the [[(Level 4) Physicist ★ ↔]] Domain Card in your loadout, roll a d10 instead of a d4.
