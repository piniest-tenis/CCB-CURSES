Gain a special long rest downtime move called _Magical Research_. When you complete a long rest and use this move, place a token on this card.

You can spend these tokens to alter an existing spell, either created  via [[(Level 4) Charlatan ★ ↔]] and [[(Level 4) Gifted ★ ↔]] or an existing spell within the Weird domain. You can spend any number of tokens to gain one of the following permanent alterations:

- **1 Token** _Long Range_: Increase the range increment of the spell
- **2 Tokens** _Afflicting_: Choose an already defined condition. This spell afflicts that condition on its target on a success.
- **4 Tokens** _Massive_: Replace damage dice on the spell with d20s.

Alternatively, you can propose an alteration to your GM who will set a cost for the requested alteration.
