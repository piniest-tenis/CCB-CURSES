Your comfortability with the supernatural has attracted a curious specimen of [[Etherotaxia]] that follows you intently. You can issue this [[Creature]] basic commands (e.g. Come, Go, Stay) and it will follow them to the best of its understanding.

After a long rest, gain a number of tokens on this card equal to your Proficiency. You may spend these tokens to activate any of the following tricks:
- **Share senses**: You gain a brief glimpse of the world through the senses of your Ethereal Buddy. When you do so, you can determine general details about a scene but specific information may be hard to decipher
- **Alert**: Your Ethereal Buddy will make a noise audible only to you if a general activity you describe to it takes place within Close range of it. 
- **Aid**: Before you roll, describe how your Ethereal Buddy sits you. Lower the Difficulty of your roll by 1.

Clear unspent tokens at the start of a long rest.
