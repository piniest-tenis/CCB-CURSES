After a long rest, gain a number of tokens equal to your level plus the amount of domain cards with a **Curse** feature in your current loadout, including this one. 

When casting a spell that would not harm a [[Creature]] or a [[Character]], you can spend a token to reduce the Difficulty of the Spellcast Roll by 5.

When casting a spell that would harm a Creature or a Character, you can spend any number of tokens after the roll to gain a +2 bonus to the result of this roll for each token spent.
***
**Curse**: You are laden with cursed items, and find them harder to shed than most. You cannot freely change domain cards with a **Curse** feature during downtime. If you have the [[(Level 3) Shadow ★ ↔]] Domain Card, after a long rest, choose a Level 4 or below domain card with a **Curse**. You may ignore the effects of the **Curse** until your next long rest.
