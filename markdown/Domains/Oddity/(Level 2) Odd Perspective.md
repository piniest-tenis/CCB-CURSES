When you are witness to a failed non-attack roll by an ally, you can spend a Hope to attempt the same action with one of the following bonuses:
- Gain a bonus to your roll equal to the difference between the roll's Difficulty and your ally's roll.
- Roll d4 using your Proficiency bonus. Take the highest result and have the target mark that much Stress.
- All allies within Close range can mark a Stress to clear a Hit Point.
