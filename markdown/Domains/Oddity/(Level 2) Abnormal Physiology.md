You develop a physical feature unusual for your Species. After a rest, choose one of the following effects that lasts until your next rest:
- _Extrasensory perception_: Once per scene, you can mark a Stress to re-roll your Fear die.
- _Punishing heft_: Once per scene when you succeed at at an attack roll, add d6 physical damage using your Proficiency.
- _Hypnotic movements:_ Once per scene, you can momentarily _Stun_ a creature that can see you within Close range. While _Stunned_, they can't use reactions and can't take any other actions until they clear this condition.
