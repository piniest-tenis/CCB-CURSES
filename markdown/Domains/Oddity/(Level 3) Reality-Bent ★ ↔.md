You have become detached from the reality around you. Once per session, work with your GM to change a minor detail about the world. If this cannot be granted, you instead place a token on this card. You may spend this token at any time to grant yourself an automatic success on one roll.

At the end of session, clear all unspent tokens.

**Curse**: The world of Tidwell is actively rejecting you. You are hunted by an entity within Tidwell who seeks to expel you from the world at all costs. You will eventually encounter this entity, and it will succeed, causing your character to no longer be playable. If you have the [[(Level 3) Universalist ★ ↔]] Domain Card, you have an opportunity to negotiate with this entity. Roll against an 18 Difficulty. On a success, the entity will agree and leave you forever.
