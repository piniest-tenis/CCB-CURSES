You gain a one-time special downtime project called _Scripture_ with a Countdown Tracker of 4. Once you start this project, you can gain a permanent +1 bonus to either you Presence, Instinct or Knowledge score. When this project completes, you can choose to gain one of the following effects permanently:
- Gain 2 [[Reputation]] bonuses. You can distribute these bonuses to any [[Faction]] whose current score Reputation is 0 or better.
- Create a new [[Faction]]. [[Adherent]]s see you as the [[Figurehead]] of this faction.
- Treat this card as if it had the following text:
  **(Level 5) Aura of Destiny**
  
  **Aura**
  
  Once per rest, you can activate Aura of Destiny. While active, allies in Close range cannot mark their last Hit Point. If a character were to mark their last Hit Point, you must mark a Stress instead. If you have no more Stress to mark, this aura effect ends immediately.
