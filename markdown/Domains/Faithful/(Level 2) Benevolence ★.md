You can spend a Hope to grant an ally a token that can be redeemed for one of the following benefits:
- Reroll a die before the result of the roll is known
- Gain Advantage on one attack
- Gain the benefits of a short rest downtime activity

---
**Curse:** When you use this ability, you are stricken with the sense that your generosity is unrepaid. You feel resentment towards the recipient until your next long rest unless they make a concerted effort to show you gratitude.
