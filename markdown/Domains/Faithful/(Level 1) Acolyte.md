Your experience in a structured belief system has left you adept at navigating their structures and orders.

After a long rest, place a number of tokens on this card equal to your Instinct. When you deal with a religious organization, you can spend a token from this card to gain one benefit from the following options:

- Gain Advantage on a roll to negotiate, persuade, or influence
- Clear a Stress
- Gain a Hope

When you take a long rest, clear all unspent tokens.
