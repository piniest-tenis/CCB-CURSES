You have gained the ability to intuit details from the land of Tidwell itself. Once per long rest, make a Spellcast Roll (13). On a success with Hope, you may learn three details about the land within Far range. On a success with Fear, you may learn one detail. Any information that transpired within the space of the land can be learned, but is confined only to what the land understands. The land may know how many creatures passed through an area, but it would not know what they were doing there. On a failure, the land ignores you.
***
**Curse:** Your attunement to the land has come at the cost of your attunement to the actual beings that inhabit it. After using this ability, you become mute until the end of the Scene. If you have the [[(Level 3) Provocateur ★ ↔]] domain card, you can imbue a command into the land. It will do everything it can to comply with that command.
