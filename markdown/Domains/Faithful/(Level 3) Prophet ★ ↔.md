**When chosen, declare the deity to whom you are bound as a prophet. This cannot be changed later.**

You gain an aura of power, and any adherent of a religion treats you more favorably. When interacting with adherents of an organized religion, you may add your tier to influence their actions or opinions.

Additionally, once per session you can make a Spellcast Roll against a [[Character]]. On a success, they abandon their current endeavor to follow you as a disciple. This effect ends when used again.

---
**Curse**: You bear the weight of responsibility as the mouthpiece of your deity. Once per session, at the GM's discretion, they may speak as your deity through you. If you have the [[(Level 3) Conman ★ ↔]] domain card, you may speak as your deity instead.
