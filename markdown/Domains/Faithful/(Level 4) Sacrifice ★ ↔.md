You are destined to be sacrificed as part of a ritual to a higher power. You may choose the higher power to whom you are sacrificed. You gain an aura of inevitability, and a number of tokens equal to your Proficiency. These tokens are refreshed at the beginning of the session.

You may spend these tokens for one of the following benefits:

- Gain advantage on any roll to endure a psychological effect
- Clear a Stress
- Gain resistance towards one type of damage until the end of the Scene

---

**Curse**: There is an hour and a day unknown to you where devotees of the higher power will expect your death in its honor. They seek your capture and will pursue you to that effect. If they manage to capture you, you will be delivered to those administering your sacrifice. If you have the [[(Level 4) Charlatan ★ ↔]] Domain Card, there is no pursuing group. Instead, you must complete a major downtime project (6 ticks) to manufacture evidence of a pursuing group. While the project is active, you must Work on this Project during a long rest or clear all tokens on this card.
