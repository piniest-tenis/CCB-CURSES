**Aura**

Once per rest, you can activate Aura of Sacrifice. While active, allies in Very Close range gain an additional Action Roll on their turn. You do not get an Action Roll, allowing you to only take actions which require no rolls. Maintenance Difficulty (15).

In addition, if a player rolls with Fear, you may dismiss the Aura immediately to change the result to be with Hope.
***
**Curse**: You develop a deep resentment for those using you. While this Aura is active, you must mark a Stress for every player in range of this Aura for for more than two turns.
