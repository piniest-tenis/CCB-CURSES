You are particularly deadly at range. After a long rest, add a number of tokens to this card equal to your Instinct. You can spend these tokens in any of the following ways:
- Add +5 to an attack roll against a target at Far range
- Add +5 damage to a damage roll against a target at Far range
- When no adversaries are at Close or Melee range, gain the Hidden condition. Attacks made from this Hidden condition gain Advantage.

When you take a long rest, clear any unspent tokens.

---
**Curse**: You've become extremely vulnerable at Close range. When you take damage from an adversary in Close or Melee range, treat it as if it hit at a damage threshold one higher (e.g. Minor => Major).

If you have the [[(Level 4) Gifted ★ ↔]] Domain Card in your loadout, you may add your Knowledge to your armor score.
