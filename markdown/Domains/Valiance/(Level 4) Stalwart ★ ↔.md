You are built to endure even the worst physical stresses. Once per scene, you may reduce the severity of incoming damage to yourself by one threshold.

---
**Curse**: When an ally within Very Close range would mark a Hit Point, you must mark 1 Stress and take it instead. If this causes you to mark your last hit point, you must choose the Blaze of Glory death move. If you have the [[(Level 4) Opportunistic ★ ↔]] Domain Card in your loadout, once per scene when you take damage in this way, you can deal half of the incoming damage back to its source immediately.
