**Aura**

Once per rest, you can activate an [[Aura]] of Destiny until the end of the scene. When the Aura of Destiny is active, any allies in Close range of you gain a +3 bonus to damage rolls. Maintenance Difficulty (13).
***
**Curse**: It is exceptionally hard for you to move inconspicuously, as you attract a small following of admirers whenever you're in public.
