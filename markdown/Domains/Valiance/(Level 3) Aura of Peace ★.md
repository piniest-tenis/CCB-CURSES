**Aura**

Once per rest, you can activate an [[Aura]] of Peace until the end of the scene. When the Aura of Peace is active, any [[Character]] within Close range of you that chooses to make an attack must mark 2 Stress. Maintenance Difficulty (15).
***
**Curse**: If you activate this ability and then later attack in the same scene, you must mark 2 additional Stress.
