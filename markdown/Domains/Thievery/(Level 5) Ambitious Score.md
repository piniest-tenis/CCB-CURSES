Gain a long rest downtime move called _Heist_. Roll a d6, and gain that many tokens on this card. During any rest, you can spend those tokens to receive any of the following stolen items:

**1 token**: Common Item/Consumable
**4 tokens**: Tier 1 Armor/Weapon or Uncommon Item/Consumable
**7 tokens**: Tier 2 Armor/Weapon or Rare Item/Consumable
**10 tokens**: Tier 3 Armor/Weapon or Legendary Item/Consumable
**13 tokens**: Tier 4 Armor/Weapon
