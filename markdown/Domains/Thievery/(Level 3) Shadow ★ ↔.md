You have become accustomed to existing unseen.

You automatically gain the _Hidden_ condition in the dark until you dismiss it explicitly or via actions.
When you succeed on an attack with Advantage against a [[Creature]] or [[Character]], spend a Hope to deal additional d8 physical damage using your Proficiency.
***
**Curse**: Your adherence to the dark has left you ill-conditioned for polite society. You take on a pallid appearance, and rolls to influence [[Character]]s are done with Disadvantage. If you have the [[(Level 4) Possessed ★ ↔]] domain card, your appearance and mannerisms are treated as a novelty. Once per session, you may ask a [[Favor]] of a friendly character. That character will do their best within reason to accommodate that request.
