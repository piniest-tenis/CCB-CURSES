You have grown proficient in accosting [[Character|Characters]] and taking valuable items from them. As part of any attack, you can roll a d4. On a four, you may steal one item from their person even if the original attack failed. If the item has already been described, you gain that item. If you are seeking an item that hasn't been described, work with your GM to create one mundane item of your choosing.
***
**Curse**: You've stolen from the wrong person. At the GM's discretion, but no more than once after choosing this domain card, you will be informed of an organization that wants to force you to work for them or to imprison or harm you. They will take actions to effect this outcome. If you have the [[(Level 4) Diplomat ★ ↔]] domain card, they will instead ingratiate themselves to you. Should you encounter them, you may instead roll a d6 and recruit the result to do your bidding at a later date.
