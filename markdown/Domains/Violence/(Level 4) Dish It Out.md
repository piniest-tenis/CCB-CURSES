**Grimoire**

**Roundhouse Smash**: Make an attack roll against all enemies within Very Close range. On a success, targets take your normal weapon damage and an additional d10 physical damage using your Proficiency. 

**Vicious Strike**: Make an attack roll with your primary weapon. On a success, gain a Hope to give your target the condition _Trauma_. Before a target affected with _Trauma_ takes any action, they take the damage received on the original attack. _Trauma_ is cleared when the affected target takes an action to clear it or they have received damage from _Trauma_ twice.
