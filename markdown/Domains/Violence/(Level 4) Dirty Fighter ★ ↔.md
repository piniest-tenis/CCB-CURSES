**Grimoire**

**Blind**: On a successful melee attack, spend a Hope to give the condition **Blind** to your target.

**Sucker Punch**: On a successful attack using Advantage, after rolling for damage, spend 2 Hope to double the total damage your target receives.

**Shell up**: On a successful melee attack targeting you, mark a Stress and gain a bonus to your damage threshold equal to your Proficiency. This effect ends after damage is resolved.

---

**Curse**: Your reputation proceeds you. When you are present, all attempts by you or an ally to de-escalate a situation gain Disadvantage. Additionally, on a successful roll to de-escalate a situation where you are present, you must mark a Stress. 

If you have the [[(Level 4) Fop ★ ↔]] domain card in your loadout, when a roll to de-escalate a situation succeeds, gain a Hope instead of marking a Stress.
