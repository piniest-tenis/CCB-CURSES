Your words are imbued with the power to fundamentally change how a [[Creature]] or [[Character]] thinks. Make a Spellcast Roll against a creature that can hear you. On a success, you can speak a thought into a target's mind which they will adopt wholeheartedly as their own. Only one such thought can be maintained at a time. This effect lasts until you dismiss it, you implant another thought in this or another target, or the target is incapacitated.
***
**Curse**: Your speech is powerful and is no longer something you can responsibly use sparingly. Spoken words are difficult for you. Mark a Stress any time you speak more than five words in a thought. If you speak more than three sentences to any one character during a scene, you gain the _Exhausted_ condition until your next rest. While _exhausted_, you may only move or take one action on your turn. Any roll that requires you to speak while _exhausted_ is made with Disadvantage.
