Gain a number of tokens on this card equal to your Presence. You can spend these tokens to gain any of the following effects:
- Add +1 bonus to the result of a roll to deceive or trick a [[Character]] into believing a lie you told them
- Add a -1 penalty Difficulty of a roll to avoid scrutiny when you are disguised as another character
