You gain a +1 bonus to your Presence as long as this card is in your loadout, and gain Advantage on rolls to de-escalate a situation. If you succeed at de-escalating a situation, place a token on this card.

You can spend tokens on this card to take a special downtime move called _Take Tea_. You can _Take Tea_ with party members who are using the _Prepare_ downtime move. You gain all the same benefits of _Preparing Together_ as well as clearing all Stress.
***
**Curse**: When you mark a Hit Point or an Armor slot, you must mark a Stress. If you have the [[(Level 3) Philosopher ★ ↔]] domain card in your loadout, any Stress marked this way is cleared at the end of the scene.
