You gain a special long rest downtime move called _Establish Contacts_. When you undertake this project, roll a number of d4s equal to your Tier. You may ask the GM a number of questions equal to the highest result, each about specific information on a topic of your choice, as long as that information is known to a Faction you are currently in contact with or to the inhabitants of your current region.
