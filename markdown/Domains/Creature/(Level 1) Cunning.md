When you roll with Fear, place a token on this card. You can spend tokens from this card to gain one of the following effects:
- Add +1 to the result of an Instinct roll.
- While out of combat, gain the _Hidden_ condition.
- Add +1 to the result of a Damage roll.
After a long rest, clear any unspent tokens from this card.
