Once per rest when an adversary within Very Close range fails a roll, you may spend a Hope to make a roll against them with Advantage. Treat all successes on this roll as a _critical success_.

**Curse**: You are prone to long bouts of fasting, often through no choice of your own. The world intervenes in subtle ways to keep you from finding things easily. When you use this ability and it results in a _critical success_, your next failure is treated as a _critical failure_, and will give the GM two Fear. If you have the [[(Level 3) Psychic ★ ↔]] domain card in your loadout when this failure happens, roll a d6 and on a rolled 6 you ignore the cost.
