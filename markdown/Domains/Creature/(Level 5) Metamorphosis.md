You gain a special downtime project called _Metamorphosis_ with a Countdown Tracker of 2. This project may be undertaken multiple times. When you complete this project, you gain the _Altered_ condition which grants you a key feature or ability of a [[Creature]] you have encountered previously. You may have two active _Altered_ conditions active at one time. When you complete a _Metamorphosis_ project with two already active _Altered_ conditions, choose one to dismiss.
