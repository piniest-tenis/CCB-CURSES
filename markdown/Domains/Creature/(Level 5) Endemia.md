Once per rest, you can choose a location, region, or geography and give yourself the _Endemic_ condition. This allows you to ignore any effects inherent in the setting. For instance, an _endemic_ condition for a lake would allow you to breathe underwater, or an _endemic_ condition for [[Forestdown]] would allow you to ignore the effects of [[Creep]]. In addition, [[Creature|Creatures]] and [[Character|Characters]] that are native to the chosen location, region or geography treat you with a +1 bonus to [[Attitude]].
