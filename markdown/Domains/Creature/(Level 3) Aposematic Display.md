You develop markings that advertise to potential adversaries that you are not worth the cost of attacking. After a long rest, place a number of tokens on this card equal to your Proficiency. When a creature makes an attack roll against you, you can spend a token from this card to enact one of the following effects:

- *Threat Display*: The attack roll against you gains Disadvantage.
- *Toxic Display*: When an adversary attacking you succeeds, after you have taken damage the adversary marks a Hit Point.
- *Müllerian Display:* Gain a bonus to your Evasion equal to your Presence until the attack resolves.
Clear unused tokens on this card at the start of a long rest.
