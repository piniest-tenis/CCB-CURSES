You become adept at avoiding conflicts. Whenever you make rolls in an attempt to avoid a fight, add your Agility to the result. If you fail a roll in this manner, you may mark a Stress to re-roll.

---
**Curse**: Predatory creatures are hell-bent on eating you. If a creature that would eat you feels that could, it will try do so by entering into conflict with you. If you have the [[(Level 4) Time-Bent ★ ↔]] Domain Card in your loadout, once per Session when you fail to avoid this kind of conflict, you may rewind to the end of the previous scene.
