You are considerably more cunning than most characters, and your traits that define you as a predator become more apparent. Attacks made against you gain Disadvantage so long as you have any Hope.
***
**Curse**: You frighten prey animals. [[Character|Characters]] and [[Creature|Creatures]] which would fear the creature you have come to resemble actively distrust you. Gain a -1 penalty to the [[Attitude]] of these creatures. They will seek to either hide or defend themselves against you. If you have the [[(Level 4) Safecracker ★ ↔]] Domain Card in your loadout, you may attempt to use you heightened senses against these characters. Make a Strength or Presence roll against a Difficulty of 15. On a success, the character will fearfully oblige you and you treat their attitude as if it were +3 for the duration of the scene. On a failure, they will attempt to flee immediately.
