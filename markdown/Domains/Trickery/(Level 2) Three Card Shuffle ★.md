When you succeed a roll with Fear, you can spend 2 Hope to place a token on this card. Store the result of your successful roll, and re-roll a new result. You must use the new roll. 
You can spend the token to use that stored roll on another Action, Spellcast, or Trait roll.

At the end of this scene, clear all tokens on this card and mark 2 Stress for each token cleared this way.
***
**Curse**: You have an obviously rigged device somewhere on your person. If it is discovered by an authority or any [[Character]] who would object to you possessing this device, you must send this card to your Vault until your next rest.
