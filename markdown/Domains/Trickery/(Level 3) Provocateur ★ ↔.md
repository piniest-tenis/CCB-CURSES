You are adept at pushing the right buttons to manufacture action where it did not exist previously. Once per Scene, you can say a few words or take an action towards another character. Make a Spellcraft Roll (13). On a success with Hope, the target will endeavor to carry out an action of your choosing until the end of the Scene. On a success with Fear, the target will endeavor to carry out the described action unless they mark 2 Stress.

**Curse**: Your time spent manipulating others has left you cynical and hardened. Your cynicism has manifested in a hypervigilance. During a short rest, you can only take one downtime action. If you have the [[(Level 4) Sacrifice ★ ↔]] domain card, the sense of inevitability has brought you peace. Instead, during a short rest you may take three downtime actions.
