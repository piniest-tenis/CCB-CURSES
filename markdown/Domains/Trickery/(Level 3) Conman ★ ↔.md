You gain Advantage on all rolls to deceive [[Character|Characters]].

Additionally, once per session, you can spend 2 Hope to implant an idea in a character's mind after a few minutes of conversation. The character will believe this idea wholeheartedly until the end of the Scene.

**Curse**: There are wronged parties tracking you down to seek recompense. This manifests in a number of mildly negative and inconvenient ways when interacting with characters throughout Tidwell. If you have the [[(Level 3) Reality-Bent ★ ↔]] domain card, characters will forget they have been conned by you once the scene ends.
