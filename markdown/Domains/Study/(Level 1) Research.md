You gain a short rest downtime move called _Research_ which replaces _Prepare_. You can _Research_ with one or more members of your party who use _Prepare_ as if they were also doing _Research_, but only you gain the additional benefits.

When you _Research_, gain the normal benefits of _Prepare_ and place two tokens on this card. Any number of tokens can be spent from this card. For each spent token, you can gain one of the following effects:
- +1 bonus to the result of a Knowledge roll
- +1 bonus to the result of a Damage roll
- +1 temporary bonus to your Evasion which expires after being attacked
