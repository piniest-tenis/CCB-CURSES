**Grimoire**

You are especially adept at creating tools suited to aggressive situations.

**Deadfall Trap**: Make a Spellcast Roll (13) to create a physics-based trap within Melee range. On a success, a trap is created that will trigger based on a condition you describe. When this trap triggers, any [[Character|Characters]] or [[Creature|Creatures]] in Melee range take d20 damage using your Proficiency.

**Harpoon**: Make a Spellcast Roll against a target within Far range. On a success, deal d4 damage using your Proficiency and give your target the _Tethered_ condition with the origin as your current location. While _Tethered_, a creature or character cannot move except towards the origin of the tether. At the start of your turn, make a Strength Roll with Advantage against the _Tethered_ creature. On a success, you can pull the creature one range distance closer.
