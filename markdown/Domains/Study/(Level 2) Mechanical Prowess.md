**Grimoire**

In a pinch, you can create mechanical solutions to common problems.

**Fashion Lock**: Make a Spellcast Roll (15) on an object you can see. On a success, you can craft a lock which will only open unless conditions you specify are met. A [[Character]] can spend an hour of study time to break the lock if left alone with it.

**Mobility Device**: Make a Spellcast Roll (13). You fashion a device which gives you the ability to do one of the following:
- Fly up to Far range without a roll
- Climb up to Far range without a roll
- Swim up to Far range without a roll
Once the Mobility Device is used, it is destroyed.

**Sentry**: Make a Spellcast Roll (15). On a success, you create a device which alerts you to an activity of your description. The device alerts on any trigger activity within Close range of it. It is reasonably inconspicuous and relatively easy to hide.
