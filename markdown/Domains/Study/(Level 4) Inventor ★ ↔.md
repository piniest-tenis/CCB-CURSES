You gain an innate understanding of an advanced engineering concept, and create a device unique to your character. Work with your GM to outline the rules of this device. After a long rest, place a number of tokens equal to your Knowledge on this card. It may be used to enact one of the following effects:
- One of two effects chosen by you and agreed to by the GM
- Gain the benefits of a Short Rest action immediately

When you take a long rest, clear any unspent tokens on this card.
