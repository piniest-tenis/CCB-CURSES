You become a veritable savant at sleuthing. After a long rest, gain a number of tokens on this card equal to your Proficiency. You can spend a token to enact any of the following effects:
- Come from another angle: Add an additional trait to a trait roll when investigating.
- Interrogation: Gain a +5 bonus to a roll used to question a [[Character]]
- Dogged Pursuit: Move up to Far distance without it counting as your action for that turn

Clear unspent tokens on this card when you take a long rest.

---
**Curse**: Your new abilities and mannerisms lead others to believe you might be involved with enforcing laws or regulations. Characters invested in avoiding attention (criminals, heretics, etc) have a worsened attitude towards you and will refuse to cooperate with even the most simple requests. If you have the [[(Level 4) Burglar ★ ↔]] Domain Card in your loadout, you may spend a token from this card to gain Advantage on a roll involving a character otherwise affected by this [[Curse]].
