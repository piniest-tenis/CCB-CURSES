You gain a special downtime project called _Doomsday Device_ with a Completion Tracker of 6. While this project is underway, gain a +1 bonus to your Knowledge, Presence, or Instinct. When the project completes, choose from one of the following effects:
- Gain a single-use weapon that does d20+5 damage using your _Party's Combined Proficiency_.
- Gain a single-use piece of equipment. For one scene, all characters that are within Very Close range of the device will not take damage as long as they take no actions.

This project can only be completed once.
