You have spent a long time thinking about the big questions in life.

After a long rest, place a number of tokens equal to the number of Study domain cards in your loadout and vault on this card.

When you would make a Knowledge, Presence, or Instinct trait roll, spend any number of tokens after the roll to gain a +1 bonus for each token spent.

Whenever you critically succeed in a trait roll using Knowledge, Presence, or Instinct, place a token on this card.

When you take a long rest, clear all unspent tokens.

---
**Curse**: You are seen as insufferable by many [[Character|Characters]], especially those who have a bias towards action. When a target for a roll to influence a character is declared, the GM will inform you if the curse is active. If it is, the roll will gain Disadvantage.

If you have the [[(Level 3) Scrapper ★ ↔]] domain card in your loadout, you may spend a token from this card to negate the Disadvantage granted by the Curse.
