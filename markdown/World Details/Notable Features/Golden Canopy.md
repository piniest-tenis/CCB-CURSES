The Golden Canopy is where the senate of Reveille meets. It is a massive building carved into a standing boulder, capped by an elaborately adorned golden masterwork depicting branches of both the Tree of Life and the Tree of Strength. The interior of the building is cool, damp, and full of natural light filtered through the leaves of these sculptures. When senate is in session, the main entrance to the building is fiercely guarded. At all other times, it is open to the public.
