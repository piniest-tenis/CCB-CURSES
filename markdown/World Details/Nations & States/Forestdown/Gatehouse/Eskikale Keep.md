An ancient building made of a mix of thick granite stones and intricate [[Sallidean Stone]] carvings all across the exterior and crenels.

The keep predates the town of [[The Land of Tidwell/World Details/Nations & States/Forestdown/Gatehouse/Gatehouse]], and its builders are unknown to its current inhabitants. Through its centuries of use, numerous additions have been tacked onto the original structure, giving it an oddly patchwork character that belies its heavy fortifications.

Home to three major operational wings, and a destroyed fourth wing, as well as numerous outbuildings housing the various members of the [[Hygiane Order]].
