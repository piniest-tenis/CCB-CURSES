# Forestdown Overview
 
```ccard
type: folder_brief_live
```
 
An expansive, largely untouched forest inhabited by spirits of corruption. Home to only one city of the living, [Gatehouse](The%20Land%20of%20Tidwell/World%20Details/Nations%20&%20States/Forestdown/Gatehouse/Gatehouse.md). There is an area deep in the woods that houses what is rumored to be a city of the spirits in the forest, called [The Invisible City](The%20Invisible%20City.md)
 
Forestdown has become a place of legends, rumored to be full of [[Masked Host]] settlements and creatures unlike anywhere else in Tidwell.
