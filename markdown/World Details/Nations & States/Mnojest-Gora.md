The volcanic and mountainous region in the westernmost part of [Tidwell](Tidwell.md). The majority of [Goran](Goran.md) tribes live within this area, where they are native. This area used to possess much steeper crags, but the massive eruption of Rhodia during the appearance of [The World Book](The%20World%20Book.md) has left long flat glassy "lakes" of solidified magma flows in between the tall peaks of the Mnojest mountains. These are collectively known as [Shalki](Shalki.md).
