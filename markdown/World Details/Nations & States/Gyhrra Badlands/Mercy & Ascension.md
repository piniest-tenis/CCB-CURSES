The sister cities of the [[Church of the Revenant God]]. Mercy & Ascension sit across a vast chasm over the [[Penitent River]], joined by the impressive [[Hand of Aggemnon]].

**Mercy**
The larger of the two cities, Mercy is the seat of worldly government of the Church. Here, the [[Emerald Empress]] heads the [[Parliament of Faithful]], from which all orders of the [[Revenant God]] are pressed into law. The city, despite its strict religious regiments, is the most populous and metropolitan in all of Tidwell excepting the capital [[The Land of Tidwell/World Details/Nations & States/Carnithia/Reveille/Reveille]].

**Ascension**
The smaller city, Ascension holds the seat of the [[Alabaster Deacon]]. Unlike Mercy, Ascension bears none of the trappings of worldly acclaim present in its sister city. Instead, it holds a clutch of [[Paarlid]] clad domes in places of worship, and a vast number of low adobe homes filled with the [[Proselytids]] of the Revenant God's faith. Access to Ascension is severely limited to outsiders, and those who do visit the city must be minded by Proselytids trained specifically to help visitors keep with the customs of the town.
