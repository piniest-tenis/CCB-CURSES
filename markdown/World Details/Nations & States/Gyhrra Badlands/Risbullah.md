The largest Nation-State in the [Gyhrra Badlands](Gyhrra%20Badlands.md). Populated by a diverse group of traders, and known for its eclectic combination of architecture, arts, and fashion.

The most notable feature of Risbullah from afar is the massive [Sulahfas](Sulahfas.md) shells that make up the three main districts of the city, under which the overbearing sun is considerably more tolerable.

Risbullah is a trade port, first and foremost, with a bustling port east of the large delta of the [Alnaar River](Alnaar%20River.md)
