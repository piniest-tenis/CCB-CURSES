Once a massive floodplain, the eruption of Rhodia caused a major disruption in weather conditions on [Tidwell](Tidwell.md). The dense forests of [Forestdown](Forestdown.md) used to stretch all the way to the rocky outcroppings of Gyhrra where the frequent and copious rains would drain to the southern seas. After the eruption, the western winds were disrupted by volcanic ash, changing the air current to sweep north over the topmost edge of [Tidwell](Tidwell.md).

This left the Gyhrra largely dry, with the only evidence of the water being the deeply carved ravines and canyons and the numerous eroded hills in the shape of tall mesas. The most prominent of these is [The Southern Mesa](The%20Southern%20Mesa.md) upon which the ancient [Wishing Turtle](Wishing%20Turtle.md) is said to reside.
