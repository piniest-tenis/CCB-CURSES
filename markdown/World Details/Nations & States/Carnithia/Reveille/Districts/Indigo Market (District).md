The Indigo Market District is filled with wide stone pathways carved into the hill's surface. These are populated by numerous long carts filled with goods. More than any other good, large planks of hewn stone weigh down these carts. It is the same smooth bluish-gray stone that makes up the hill and the road itself. Otherwise, a number of large warehouses hold goods that are traded at the [Indigo Market (building)](Indigo%20Market%20(building).md) at the heart of the district.
