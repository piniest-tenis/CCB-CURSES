A community of schools and large dormitories where the tradespeople of Reveille learn, apprentice and live with one another. Headed by three powerful schools, the [Medicamentia](Medicamentia.md), the [Physicamentia](Physicamentia.md) and the [Etherementia](Etherementia.md).
 
The roads here are narrower, and [Teetle Cart](Teetle%20Cart.md)s aren't allowed in the district at all. A long gate of wrought iron and stone separates this area from the [Common District](Common%20District.md) and the [Salt District](Salt%20District.md). There are two monitored gates, with a security force paid by the three schools.
 
This area is consistently lit in the evenings, and has activity all throughout the day and night, as the [Etherementia](Etherementia.md) is generally held only after sunset.
