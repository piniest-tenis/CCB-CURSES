Inhabited by the majority of the population of the city. Here, you will find a number of stores, homes, schools, various businesses catering to day-to-day life. The [Zoning Commission](Zoning%20Commission.md) sets regulations within this community, though they are fiercely opposed by the [Free Citizens Society](Free%20Citizens%20Society.md). The [Children's Hope Organization](Children's%20Hope%20Organization.md) benefits from the Foundling homes set aside by the [Zoning Commission](Zoning%20Commission.md), but otherwise stay apolitical in the struggle between the two.
