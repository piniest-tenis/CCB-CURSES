The largely abandoned religious grounds at the peak of [Danitra Hill](Danitra%20Hill.md). In deep disrepair, this area is dotted with eroded sculptures dedicated to unknown figures in a language that's indiscernible to modern viewers. The ground is hypersaturated despite the altitude and the air is dank with the smell of decay.￼￼A number of buildings in various stages of falling down dot the landscape, here, though save for a few obvious homes their original purposes are unknown. It is believed to be haunted. ￼￼To one side of this peak, there lies the entire city of Reveille, heading downslope to the extensive wheat and fruit orchards of the plain beyond.￼￼On the other, there are steep drop-offs to the Rohis and Guernsa Rivers where they meet to become the Meristol River heading out to the fjords of Mnojest-Gora.
