A harsh briny scent of dried meats and sealife hangs in the air here, and walking the streets causes a fine white salt dust to accumulate on everything. The salt mines far underneath [Danitra Hill](Danitra%20Hill.md) have their entrances here, and the storehouses along the main thoroughfare use this to their advantage. Food is shipped from all over Tidwell to be preserved for long-term storage here, as salt is an exceedingly rare resource to find in quantity in Tidwell, with the only other options being to refine it from the [Hardmill Kabir Basin](Hardmill%20Kabir%20Basin.md) in the [Gyhrra Badlands](Gyhrra%20Badlands.md)
