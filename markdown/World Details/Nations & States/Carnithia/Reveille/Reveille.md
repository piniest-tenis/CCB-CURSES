The capital of [Tidwell](Tidwell.md) and the confederacy of [Carnithia 1](Carnithia%201.md)
 
A large city built around a central monastery that sits mostly abandoned. This monastery is known informally as [The Garden](The%20Garden.md). At the center of the monastery sits the abandoned and disrepaired temple which houses the [Storied Altar](Storied%20Altar.md). This is a testament to the state of Reveille and indeed Tidwell as a whole at the sunset of the Fifth Age.
 
Ringing the [Storied Altar](Storied%20Altar.md) is a hill carved into stepstones. These once were benches in an amphitheater, though whether this served as a place of worship is dubious. There is a too-green stink about the entire monastery, as if there are layers of decaying moss hidden in every corner.
 
Large carved stone figures sit at odd angles, none quite perpendicular to the ground. Time and weather have eroded any discernible features, leaving them to appear as statues of ghosts.
 
A thick stone wall bearing the signs of many hasty repairs rings the central courtyard. Buildings in various states of repair are dotted throughout this space, but only the Storied Altar gives any hint as to what its original purpose is. Despite the open space, none of the cityfolk dare make their home inside the monastery as rumors of its haunting are muttered about by serious people in dingy taverns all throughout Reveille.
 
Even outside the damaged walls, only the most unstable of the city's residents would call home. This is where the [Church of the Bent Trunk](Church%20of%20the%20Bent%20Trunk.md) operates, under the guidance of its chapterhouse leader [Necker](Necker.md).
 
Down slope from this is the city proper. It is behind a much more well maintained wall than the monastery. The sounds and smells of people and beasts of burden overwhelm this space. This area closest to [The Garden](The%20Garden.md) is dominated by transport and logistics houses which make up the [Indigo Market (District)](Indigo%20Market%20(District).md).

Further down the hill, where the ground becomes more even, is the [Patrician Grounds](Patrician%20Grounds.md) and the [Golden Canopy](Golden%20Canopy.md).
Beyond that, the food preservation industry thrives in the [Salt District](Salt%20District.md). This is an area controlled by two forces, the [Packing Union](Packing%20Union.md) and the [Hubbet Mining Corporation](Hubbet%20Mining%20Corporation.md)
Further down slope, where there is barely a rise any longer, lies the [Common District](Common%20District.md), and the [Guild Houses](Guild%20Houses.md).
In the plains surrounding [Danitra Hill](Danitra%20Hill.md) lies the largest and lease densely populated district, [The Fallows](The%20Fallows.md)
