The wet season which renders most of the surrounding area of the [[The Land of Tidwell/World Details/Nations & States/The Waste/Varjalune Republic/Varjalune Republic]] impassable. Rain is frequent, occurring 9 of every 10 days, and sunlight is scarce. It is a time generally associated with long periods indoors, gathered around fires. Also known as the Feast Season and the Season of Sorrow.
