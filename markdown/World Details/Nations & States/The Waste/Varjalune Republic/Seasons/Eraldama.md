Dry Kuivatuul winds begin blowing in from [The Waste](The%20Waste.md), as the weather patterns shift from Westerly to Easterly. The Easterly winds (Kuivatuul) bring in dry air from the northern edge of The Waste. This drying period is a crucial time for Varjalune, as it opens up the trade routes to the interior of Tidwell. This period is when Alipinn gathers the resources it needs from the more temperate [[The Land of Tidwell/World Details/Geographic Features/Carnithia/Carnithia|Carnithia]] and [[Gyhrra Badlands]] communities. This is largely paid for by the sale of goods from Kybon and the harvesting of [[Paarlid]].
