The only major nation-state of The Wastes, though it's only by technicality they're located there. They sit on the border between Forestdown, Carnithia and The Wastes, and since Carnithia doesn't lay a claim to the cities of Alipinn and Taliga, they've been classified as part of The Wastes merely due to proximity to the much wilder area of the continent.

A thriving trade Republic, mainly due to their access to trade with the [[The Land of Tidwell/Daggerheart Homebrew/Communities/Kybon Isle|Kybon Isle]] and the unique and sought-after goods which arrive from there, even if land trade to the Republic is feasible only during the [[Vihmarood]].
