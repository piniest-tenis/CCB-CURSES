The vast and varied landscape on the Eastern edge of [Tidwell](Tidwell.md) inhabited by the largest known creatures on the continent. The Waste is largely unexplored due to the dangers for most of the civilized populations of Tidwell, though there is a rumored town called [Bogdown](The%20Land%20of%20Tidwell/World%20Details/Nations%20&%20States/The%20Waste/Bogdown.md) somewhere deep in its interior.￼￼Much of the landscape that has been explored is gloamy wetlands, peppered with unexplained large hills that exist in seeming isolation.
