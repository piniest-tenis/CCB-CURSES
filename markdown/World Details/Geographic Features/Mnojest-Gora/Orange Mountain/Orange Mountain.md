A massive rust-colored mountain in [[The Land of Tidwell/World Details/Nations & States/Mnojest-Gora]] which holds a particular religious significance to the [[Goran]] peoples. Gorans are expected to make a trip to the [[The Land of Tidwell/World Details/Geographic Features/Mnojest-Gora/Orange Mountain/Orange Mountain|Orange Mountain]] at some point in their lives, and to spend a year there contemplating the bedrock pushed up from the depths below Tidwell.

A large, corvid figure, known as the [[Raven Apostle]] had appeared mysteriously sometime in the 4th age, but little is known of its provenance and Gorans are strangely unaffected by its presence on their holy site.
