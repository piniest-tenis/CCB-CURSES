The widest and deepest canyon in the [Gyhrra Badlands](Gyhrra%20Badlands.md). The higher parts of the canyon are narrow and dry, but where the canyon sits the lowest it is completely filled with the [Buhadid Lake](Buhadid%20Lake.md). The canyon narrows at its southwestern edge where it empties into the [Alnaar River](Alnaar%20River.md), creating an intense rapids but also serving to filter much of the heavier iron out of the water before it hits the [Eadba Lake](Eadba%20Lake.md), the largest source of fresh water in the [Gyhrra Badlands](Gyhrra%20Badlands.md) other than the sea itself.
