A freshwater lake that is part of the [Alnaar River](Alnaar%20River.md) waterway. This lake retains the pinkish hue of iron that causes the [Buhadid Lake](Buhadid%20Lake.md) to be a deep red, but is perfectly drinkable and safe for consumption by characters and creatures alike. There are rumors that the lake serves as a creche for some of the larger creatures that live in the southern seas.
