An expansive Mesa that stands alone in an area of deep canyons in central [Gyhrra Badlands 1](Gyhrra%20Badlands%201.md). It is the rumored domain of the [Wishing Turtle](Wishing%20Turtle.md). Otherwise populated by a number of carrion creatures and ambush predators, as well as a few massive tortoise like filter feeders known as [Sulahfas](The%20Land%20of%20Tidwell/Bestiary/Creatures/Sulahfas/Sulahfas.md).
