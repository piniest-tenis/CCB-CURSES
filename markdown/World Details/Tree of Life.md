A small, twisted Oak tree that grows deep in Mnojest-Gora. It is one of the few trees deep in the mountains, and it grows inexplicably nestled in a cluster of crags surrounded by [Shalki](Shalki.md). It is large, but low-lying, and carries blooms and fruit the year round. Revered by the species of Tidwell, but seen as a threat by the [Goran](Goran.md) tribes that live in the surrounding mountains.
