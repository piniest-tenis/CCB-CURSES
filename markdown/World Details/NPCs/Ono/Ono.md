Old, somber, but more than anything confused by how much the world has changed around him.
Heavily matted and thick gray-brown fur. Impressively muscled. Deceptively soft eyes sitting behind a maw snarled by scars. Wears an assemblage of exotic furs and gifted cloaks, leading his clothing to look ragtag and unplanned.

He was a heroic figure of the first age, though he refuses to discuss why. Rumors abound that he beat back the [[Masked Host]] and moved them deep into the wilds.

He refuses to become involved in the politics of Tidwell. He is deeply detached from the day-to-day life of its denizens, and often seems distant and unapproachable.
