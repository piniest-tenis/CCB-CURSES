Magic as it's understood in Tidwell is fueled by curses. A curse is the sharp edge of a wish turned back on the wisher.

Dispelling or removing curses can be simple or near impossible based on the power of the wish. They are never destroyed, however, and only unbound from their original bearer. They persist on in the form of trinkets and talismans. Each one of these collected objects bears a boon and a curse.

The most skilled practitioners of magic in Tidwell are the Weird. They push on the edges of a shared understanding of reality, and thus are able to do things that appear as magic to the uninitiated. These abilities are often unbound by curses. However, the most powerful Weird weave the existing magic of curses into their own abilities often at great price to themselves.

For the rest, curses are the only access to anything remotely supernatural. It is rare to find entities able to grant wishes, and almost all are loathe to do so. The Forerunners were all confirmed to possess the power, including the three still living — [[Ono]], the [[Wishing Turtle]] and even [[The Spirit Ichida]], the dreaded sovereign of [[Forestdown]].
