Followers of the [[Church of the Revenant God]] see their deity as a **seven year old human boy named Dave**.

Dave has incepted the peoples of Tidwell by drawing them in his sketchpad, and has breathed life into them via his dreams of their adventures. When the world is in turmoil, Dave slumbers fitfully and risks awakening. When he is calmed, the world operates peacefully and beautifully.

Davites believe that Dave awakes at the end of an age, and his memory of the dream fades and distorts. When he dreams of Tidwell again, he brings new experiences and lives to the continent, and fills it with joy and wonder.
