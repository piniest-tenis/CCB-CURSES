The Church of the [[Revenant God]] is the oldest and most widely followed religious order in Tidwell. Its adherents can be found throughout Tidwell, often bearing the green and white stoles that mark them as such.

Non-believers ridicule the church, especially given the nature of their beliefs, but adherents rarely draw issue with this as a vast number of their rank were once amongst those very same people giving it scorn.

The strict rules of [[The Land of Tidwell/World Details/Nations & States/Gyhrra Badlands/Mercy & Ascension]] do not bind most practitioners of the Church unless they are promised to the [[Revenant God]] as tribute. 

"[[Davites]]" — as followers are known colloquially and so called due to their holy text being [[The Dave]] — are encouraged to do works of good and to endure even the most harsh injustices in order to prepare themselves for the eventual awakening of their lord. Davites are also tasked with being a calming and steady presence, and to help in times of great need, in order to lull the Revenant God to sleep so that the world might continue longer.

Ruled by the [[Petrarch]], a seven year old child chosen from the community to serve as the embodiment of the [[Revenant God]] until their eighth birthday.
