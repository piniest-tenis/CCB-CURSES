The order of knights, clerics and the weird responsible for dealing with the greater threats of Forestdown. These are major haunts, corrupted creatures and abominations. The Hospitallers are the oldest and most widely known sect of the [[Hygiane Order]], and most people believe all members of the Order to be Hospitallers.

They possess extensive training and combat experience dealing with both the [[Creep]] and the [[Etherotaxia]] of [[Forestdown]]. Historically the first line of defense against major incursions.

Today, the Hospitallers are made up mostly of older devotees of the Order, and the wing is half capable and competent soldiers and half aged and infirm veterans that would have been retired long ago in more prosperous times for the Hygiane Order.
