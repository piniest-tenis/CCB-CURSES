The Hygiane Order is tasked with tending and maintaining the corruption of [Forestdown](Forestdown.md). 

The Hygiane Order has some opposition within [[Tidwell]]'s polite society, such as [[Church of the Bent Trunk]] and the [[Church of the Revenant God]], though their opposition is generally in philosophy only. The benefit of keeping the [[Creep]] contained is hard to dispute, even for its detractors.

The Order is nearly as old as its age. It's force presentation has waxed and waned throughout the centuries, depending on both the aggression of the Creep and the political climates of Tidwell at large. For the past two centuries, the majority of the Order's support has come from [[The Waste]]'s predominant settlements, the [[The Land of Tidwell/World Details/Nations & States/The Waste/Varjalune Republic/Varjalune Republic]]. This support stems from the [[Eraldama Incursion]] incident that nearly consumed [[Taliga]].

At this point in the [[Fifth Age]], the Hygiane Order finds itself at a decidedly dangerous inflection point. The Order is the weakest it's been in living memory, and the populace of Tidwell has been consumed with the arrival of the [[Order of the Sixth Form]], a little known organization headed by an [[Isdaan]] claiming to be the [[Herald of a New Age]].
