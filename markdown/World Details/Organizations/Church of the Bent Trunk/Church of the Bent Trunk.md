Core beliefs: that the Creator's saved people are those who don't fit into society.  
Patronage: mentally ill, infirm, poor, immigrants, homeless and disfigured  
Leader: [[Gwennet Targe]], a [[Lothrian]] pygmy afflicted with a wasting disease  
Notable figures: [[Necker]]

The Church of the Bent Trunk exists wherever the poor or maladapted exist in Tidwell. As such, a chapter can be found wherever people are in Tidwell. Their teachings are simple, and their faith is one of peace and charity.

The Church adheres firmly to the [[Three Beliefs]], and preaches them in services that involve distributing donated goods and warm food to the attendees.

Most who come to the church do so because they feel some part of their life is broken. These may be visible or invisible scars that they bear, or struggles which have left them feeling overwhelmed and beaten. The Church's policy of welcoming all, and caring fiercely and fairly for all its constituents has lead to a generally positive (if piteous) view of the Church and its adherents. 

The Church recites [[The Parable of The Two Trees]] to all new recruits, and it is a widely known as a way to identify - and often mock - proselytizers of the Bent Trunk.
