1. **Struggle**
   >No creature survives without struggle; the more struggle you have encountered in life, the more worthy your soul. Seeking struggle is vanity, but accepting struggle that has found you is strength.
2. **Justice**
   >Direct action against those that have wronged you is shameful, but justice is due all to those that harm the less fortunate. The universe is uncaring, but balanced. What may feel disadvantageous now may feel advantageous later.
3. **Community**
   >There is no Church without community, and no community without service to the Church's constituents. The community gives what it can to the Church, and the Church in kind ensures that no member of the community suffers unnecessarily.
