A body of 12 members, with two for each of the [Districts](Districts.md) of [Reveille](The%20Land%20of%20Tidwell/World%20Details/Nations%20&%20States/Carnithia/Reveille/Reveille.md). There are two empty seats held for the district of [The Garden](The%20Garden.md), though no one has been elected to those seats for as long as anyone can remember. [Ono](Ono.md) has been offered a seat on multiple occasions to represent [The Garden](The%20Garden.md), but he refuses to become involved in politics.
