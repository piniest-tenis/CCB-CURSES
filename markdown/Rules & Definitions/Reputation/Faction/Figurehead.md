A [[Faction]] figurehead is a non-exclusive role for the leader of a faction. Leaders of a [[Faction]] gain a number of unique influences on the [[Attitude]] of faction's [[Adherent]]s. The scope of this influence depends largely on the type of faction.

A figurehead is responsible for setting and upholding the [[Primary Goals]] of the faction, though some faction goals are inherent to the faction and cannot be changed.

As a player, if you become a figurehead you should work with your GM to craft the nature of the relationship between yourself and the adherents of your faction.

In general, adherents of a faction will follow the direction of their figurehead to the best of their ability.
