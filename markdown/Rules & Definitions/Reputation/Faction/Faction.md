A group within Tidwell who share a common goal, belief, or custom which binds them together. Most [[Character|Characters]] and some [[Creature|Creatures]] are aligned with a faction. Factions vary greatly in size, but will always have enough constituents to act as a group and be bound by the faction's [[Primary Goals]]

An individual member of a faction are referred to as [[Adherent]]s.

Some examples of a Faction include the [[Hygiane Order]] and the [[Davites]]. Rarely, a faction may be destroyed via actions or circumstances in the story.
