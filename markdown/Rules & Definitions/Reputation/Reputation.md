Reputation is a score from -3 to 3 which denotes your standing with a [[Faction]]. 

Most unmet Factions will start a 0 score. Reputation can be earned in a variety of ways, such as by completing tasks which benefit the Faction, satisfying certain conditions, or via other special means available to some domains (such as [[(Level 4) Orator ★ ↔]] in the Artistry Domain).

Reputation determines how a Faction treats the party, what actions are available to the party with that Faction, and how willing members of the Faction are in aiding the party in their requests. Reputation also determines the base [[Attitude]] of members of the faction. Other factors may cause individual [[Character]]s within that faction to have different attitude scores towards the party or individual characters.

The party gains reputation as a group, and loses it in the same manner. Some Domain Cards may grant additional benefits to a [[Player]]'s reputation score, while a [[Curse]] might reduce or limit the reputation score a Player can have with a faction.

Party Reputation is tracked by the GM, but is generally not kept secret as long as the group's existence is known to the party.
