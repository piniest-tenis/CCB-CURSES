The baseline demeanor of an [[Adherent]] towards the party or a [[Player]].

**+6**: Will sacrifice their wellbeing and life for your goals.
**+5**: Will go out of their way to aid you in any way possible.
**+4**: Willingly offers shelter, equipment and sanctuary.
***
**+3**: Treats the party as an ally, and will defend them fervently.
**+2**: Treats the party in a friendly manner, and will offer nominal aid and assistance.
**+1**: Treats the party as trustworthy, and will listen to arguments and appeals.
***
**0**: Neutral
***
**-1**: Treats the party as untrustworthy, and will refuse basic arguments and appeals.
**-2**: Treats the party as an adversary, and sees their continued existence as a threat.
**-3**: Treats the party as a sworn enemy, and will seek to eliminate the party as a [[Primary Goals|Primary Goal]].
***
**-4**: Will harm others if it harms the party directly.
**-5**: Will harm others if it harms the party indirectly.
**-6**: Will sacrifice their wellbeing and life in order to harm the party.
