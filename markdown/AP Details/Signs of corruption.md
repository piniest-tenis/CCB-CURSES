Corruption manifests as a curse. Its negative effects are listed below.

The positive effects are unknown, but given that it is a curse there is an implication that at least one exists.
 
Initial Signs

- Excessive salivation
- Progressively worsening itching
- Unusual changes in temperature

Medial Stages

- Intense muscle aches
- Broken and lost teeth
- Lockjaw
- Intense odor of petrichor
- Delusions

Final Stages

- Changes to body morphology
    - Loss of limbs, growth of limbs, change in composition of body
- Loss of identity
- Death
