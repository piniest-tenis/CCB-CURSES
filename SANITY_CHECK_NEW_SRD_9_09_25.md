### 2.2 Resource Systems

#### HP (Hit Points) Calculation
- **Base:** Class-determined (ranges 5-7)
- **Starting Level:** 1 HP
- **Damage Threshold Formula:** `Character Level + Armor's Base Threshold`
- **Max HP per tier:**
  - Tier 1 (Level 1): 1 base + advancements
  - Tier 2-4: Up to 12 maximum
- **Damage Severity System:**
  - **Minor:** 1 HP marked
  - **Major:** 2 HP marked
  - **Severe:** 3 HP marked
  - **Massive Damage (optional):** 4 HP if damage ≥ 2× Severe threshold

#### Stress (Stress Points) Calculation
- **Base:** 6 for all classes
- **Can be increased by:**
  - Some ancestry features (e.g., Human +1, Vengeance Guardian +1)
  - Advancements
  - Class features
- **Max Stress:** 12
- **When all marked:** Becomes **Vulnerable** until clears 1+ Stress
- **If must mark but can't:** Mark 1 HP instead

#### Hope (Metacurrency)
- **Starting:** 2 per character
- **Maximum:** 6 per character
- **Carries between sessions:** YES
- **Generated by:** Success with Hope rolls, Critical Successes
- **Spent on:**
  - Help an Ally (1 Hope, roll d6 advantage die)
  - Utilize an Experience (1 Hope per Experience)
  - Initiate Tag Team Roll (3 Hope)
  - Activate Hope Features (varies, typically 3 Hope for class feature)
  - Other features as specified

#### Armor Score
- **Base:** Armor-determined (0-17)
- **Modifications:** +1 per Proficiency bonus, other features
- **Maximum:** 12
- **Armor Slots:** Can mark 1 slot to reduce damage severity by one threshold

### 2.3 Proficiency & Damage

**Proficiency Progression:**
- **Level 1:** Proficiency 1
- **Level 2:** Proficiency 2 (tier achievement)
- **Levels 3-4:** Proficiency 2
- **Level 5:** Proficiency 3 (tier achievement)
- **Levels 6-7:** Proficiency 3
- **Level 8:** Proficiency 4 (tier achievement)
- **Levels 9-10:** Proficiency 4

**Damage Formula:**
- Weapon damage: `[Proficiency] d[weapon_die] + [weapon_modifier]`
- **Example:** Proficiency 2 with Longsword (d10+3) = `2d10+3`
- **Critical Damage:** Roll damage normally, **add maximum possible die result**
  - Example: `2d10+3` critical = `2d10 + 10 + 10 + 3 = max rolled + 20 + 3`

### 2.4 Evasion & Attack Rolls

**Evasion Calculation:**
- **Base:** Class-determined (9-12)
- **Modifications:** 
  - Armor penalties (e.g., Full Plate -2)
  - Domain cards
  - Conditions
- **Against Attacks:** Difficulty = Target's Evasion
- **Can be improved by:** Domain cards, features, temporary conditions

**Attack Roll Outcome:**
- **Success with Hope:** Roll Duality Dice, Hope die > Fear die, total ≥ Difficulty → gain Hope
- **Success with Fear:** Roll Duality Dice, Fear die > Hope die, total ≥ Difficulty → GM gains Fear
- **Failure with Hope:** Total < Difficulty, Hope die > Fear die → gain Hope, GM spotlight
- **Failure with Fear:** Total < Difficulty, Fear die > Hope die → GM gains Fear + GM spotlight
- **Critical Success:** Matching Duality Dice → automatic success, gain Hope, clear Stress, +max die to damage

**Domain Card Loadout Rules:**
- **Loadout Limit:** 5 cards maximum
- **Acquisition:**
  - Character creation: 2 domain cards (can mix from one or both domains)
  - Each level-up: 1 additional domain card (level ≤ current level)
  - Multiclass: 1 domain card at ≤ half current level (rounded up)
- **Vault:** Cards beyond 5 go to vault
- **Recall Cost:** Stress cost to move card from vault to loadout during play (free during downtime)

**Domain Card Anatomy (SRD page 5):**
- **Level:** Top left (1-10)
- **Domain Symbol:** Below level
- **Recall Cost:** Top right with lightning bolt
- **Title:** Center
- **Type:** Abilities, Spells, or Grimoires
- **Feature:** Main text

### 2.6 Leveling & Advancement (SRD page 22-23)

**Tier Structure:**
- **Tier 1:** Level 1 only
- **Tier 2:** Levels 2-4
- **Tier 3:** Levels 5-7
- **Tier 4:** Levels 8-10

**Tier Achievements (automatic):**
- **Level 2:** +1 Experience (+2 modifier), +1 Proficiency
- **Level 5:** +1 Experience (+2 modifier), +1 Proficiency, clear marked traits
- **Level 8:** +1 Experience (+2 modifier), +1 Proficiency, clear marked traits

**Advancements (choose 2 per level-up from tier or below):**
1. **Increase two traits:** +1 permanent (mark until tier achievement clears)
2. **Add HP slot:** Darken outline in Hit Point section
3. **Add Stress slot:** Darken outline in Stress section
4. **Increase Experience:** +1 to two Experiences (permanent)
5. **Take domain card:** Level ≤ current level, from class's domains (or multiclass)
6. **Increase Evasion:** +1 permanent
7. **Upgrade subclass card:** Foundation → Specialization → Mastery (locks out multiclass this tier)
8. **Increase Proficiency:** Requires 2 advancement slots (one slot type)
9. **Multiclass:** Requires 2 advancement slots (one slot type), locks out subclass upgrade

**Multiclassing Rules (SRD page 23):**
- Available: Starting Level 5
- **Action:** Choose new class, select one of its two domains, gain class feature
- **Subclass:** Choose foundation card from multiclass subclasses
- **Domain Access:** Can choose cards ≤ half current level (rounded up) from multiclass domain
- **Spellcast:** If different Spellcast traits, player chooses which applies
- **Restrictions:** Locks out subclass upgrade option in that tier AND all future multiclass options

**Damage Threshold Advancement:**
- **Each level-up:** Increase all damage thresholds by 1
- **Formula:** `Level + Armor Base Threshold`

### 2.7 Combat Mechanics

**Attacking:**
- **Action Roll Type:** Specialized trait roll
- **Trait:** Specified by weapon or spell
- **Difficulty:** Target's Evasion
- **Damage Roll:** On successful attack roll
  - `[Proficiency] d[damage_die] + [modifiers]`
  - Proficiency multiplies **only** the number of dice, not flat modifiers
  - Example: Proficiency 2, weapon d6+1 = `2d6+1` (not `2d6+2`)

**Ranges:**
- **Melee:** Close enough to touch, up to a few feet
- **Very Close:** 5-10 feet (1-3 inches on map)
- **Close:** 10-30 feet (5-6 inches on map)
- **Far:** 30-100 feet (11-12 inches on map)
- **Very Far:** 100-300 feet (13+ inches on map)
- **Out of Range:** Beyond Very Far

**Movement Under Pressure:**
- As part of an action roll: Move within Close range free
- Without action roll: Make Agility Roll to move farther than Close range
- Adversary movement: Free within Close range per action, or Very Far as separate action

**Conditions (SRD page 21):**
- **Hidden:** Out of sight from all enemies; attacks against you have disadvantage; lost when seen, move into line of sight, or make attack
- **Restrained:** Can't move, can still act from current position
- **Vulnerable:** All rolls targeting you have advantage
- **Cloaked (Rogue specific):** Enhanced Hidden; remain unseen if stationary; lost after attack or end move in line of sight
- **Temporary Tags:** Creature can make move to clear (usually requires successful action roll)
- **Special Conditions:** Only cleared when specific requirements met (defined by applying effect)

**Damage Types:**
- **Physical (phy):** Standard weapon damage
- **Magic (mag):** Spell damage; weapons require Spellcast trait
- **Resistance:** Reduce damage by half before comparing to thresholds
- **Immunity:** Ignore that damage type
- **Direct Damage:** Can't be reduced by marking Armor Slots

## SECTION 3: SPECIFIC SYSTEM DETAILS BY CLASS

### 3.1 Bard (SRD page 5-6)

**Domains:** Grace & Codex  
**Starting Evasion:** 10  
**Starting HP:** 5  
**Rally Die:** d6 at level 1 → d8 at level 5

**Class Feature - Rally:**
- Once per session
- Gives Rally Die to self and all allies
- Rally Die can be spent to: roll to action/reaction/damage roll, OR clear Stress equal to result
- Clears end of session if unspent

**Hope Feature - Make a Scene (3 Hope):**
- Distract target within Close range
- Target gets -2 penalty to Difficulty

**Subclasses:**

**Troubadour (Presence trait):**
- **Foundation:** Gifted Performer
  - 3 songs, once each per long rest (can choose different targets):
    - Relaxing Song: Self + allies at Close range clear 1 HP
    - Epic Song: Target at Close range becomes temporarily Vulnerable
    - Heartbreaking Song: Self + allies at Close range gain 1 Hope
- **Specialization:** Maestro
  - When giving Rally Die to ally: Can gain Hope or clear Stress
- **Mastery:** Virtuoso
  - Perform each Gifted Performer song twice per long rest

**Wordsmith (Presence trait):**
- **Foundation:** 
  - Rousing Speech: Once per long rest, all allies within Far range clear 2 Stress
  - Heart of a Poet: After impress/persuade/offend roll, spend Hope to add d4 to roll
- **Specialization:** Eloquent
  - Once per session when encourage ally: allow mundane object find, Help Ally free, or give +1 downtime move
- **Mastery:** Epic Poetry
  - Rally Die → d10
  - Help an Ally: Can narrate as memoir, roll d10 as advantage die

### 3.2 Druid (SRD page 6-8)

**Domains:** Sage & Arcana  
**Starting Evasion:** 10  
**Starting HP:** 6  
**Beastform:** Mark Stress to transform

**Class Features:**

**Beastform:**
- Mark Stress to transform into creature of tier ≤ current tier
- Can't use weapons/domain spells in form
- Can talk, move, use other abilities
- Gain Beastform features, add Evasion bonus, use specified trait for attacks
- Armor becomes body part; Armor Slots stay marked on exit
- Drop if mark last HP

**Wildtouch:**
- Harmless nature effects at will (flowers, wind, campfire)

**Beastforms by Tier:**
- **Tier 1 (Level 1):** 6 forms (Agile Scout, Household Friend, Nimble Grazer, Pack Predator, Aquatic Scout, Stalking Arachnid)
- **Tier 2 (Levels 2-4):** 6 forms + improvements (Armored Sentry, Powerful Beast, Mighty Strider, Striking Serpent, Pouncing Predator, Winged Beast)
- **Tier 3 (Levels 5-7):** 6 forms (Great Predator, Mighty Lizard, Great Winged Beast, Aquatic Predator, Legendary Beast, Legendary Hybrid)
- **Tier 4 (Levels 8-10):** 6 forms (Massive Behemoth, Terrible Lizard, Mythic Aerial Hunter, Epic Aquatic Beast, Mythic Beast, Mythic Hybrid)

**Hope Feature - Evolution (3 Hope):**
- Transform into Beastform without marking Stress
- Choose one trait to raise by +1 until drop form

**Subclasses:**

**Warden of the Elements (Instinct trait):**
- **Foundation:** Elemental Incarnation
  - Mark Stress to Channel element (Fire/Earth/Water/Air) until Severe damage or next rest
  - Fire: Adversaries within Melee range that deal damage to you take 1d10 magic damage
  - Earth: Bonus to damage thresholds = Proficiency
  - Water: Adversaries within Melee range you deal damage to; all others at Very Close range must mark Stress
  - Air: Can hover, advantage on Agility Rolls
- **Specialization:** Elemental Aura
  - Once per rest while Channeling, assume aura affecting Close range until Channeling ends
  - Fire: Adversary marking 1+ HP also marks Stress
  - Earth: Allies gain +1 to Strength
  - Water: When adversary deals damage, mark Stress to move them to Very Close range
  - Air: When you/ally takes damage beyond Melee range, reduce by 1d8
- **Mastery:** Elemental Dominion
  - While Channeling:
    - Fire: +1 to Proficiency for damage attacks/spells
    - Earth: Roll d6 per HP marked; 6s reduce HP marked by 1
    - Water: On successful attack against you, mark Stress to make attacker Vulnerable
    - Air: +1 Evasion, can fly

**Warden of Renewal (Instinct trait):**
- **Foundation:**
  - Clarity of Nature: Once per long rest, create serenity space within Close range; spend time resting to clear Stress = Instinct (distribute between self/allies)
  - Regeneration: Touch creature, spend 3 Hope, they clear 1d4 HP
- **Specialization:**
  - Regenerative Reach: Target creatures at Very Close range with Regeneration
  - Warden's Protection: Once per long rest, spend 2 Hope to clear 2 HP on 1d4 allies at Close range
- **Mastery:** Defender
  - In Beastform, when ally at Close range marks 2+ HP, mark Stress to reduce by 1

### 3.3 Guardian (SRD page 9)

**Domains:** Blade & Valor  
**Starting Evasion:** 9  
**Starting HP:** 7  
**Unstoppable Die:** d4 at level 1 → d6 at level 5

**Class Feature - Unstoppable:**
- Once per long rest, become Unstoppable (gain Unstoppable Die)
- Start at 1, increase by 1 after each attack that deals 1+ HP
- Remove when exceeds max or scene ends
- **Benefits while Unstoppable:**
  - Reduce physical damage severity by one threshold
  - Add Unstoppable Die value to damage rolls
  - Can't be Restrained or Vulnerable

**Hope Feature - Frontline Tank (3 Hope):**
- Clear 2 Armor Slots

**Subclasses:**

**Stalwart (Armor-focused):**
- **Foundation:**
  - Unwavering: +1 permanent to damage thresholds
  - Iron Will: When taking physical damage, can mark additional Armor Slot to reduce severity
- **Specialization:**
  - Unrelenting: +2 permanent to damage thresholds
  - Partners-in-Arms: When ally at Very Close range takes damage, mark Armor Slot to reduce by one threshold
- **Mastery:**
  - Undaunted: +3 permanent to damage thresholds
  - Loyal Protector: Once per long rest, when ally at Close range has ≤2 HP and would take damage, mark Stress to sprint and take damage instead

**Vengeance (Retaliation-focused):**
- **Foundation:**
  - At Ease: +1 additional Stress slot
  - Revenge: When adversary at Melee range succeeds on attack against you, mark 2 Stress to force attacker to mark 1 HP
- **Specialization:**
  - Act of Reprisal: When adversary damages ally at Melee range, gain +1 to Proficiency on next successful attack against that adversary
- **Mastery:**
  - Nemesis: Spend 2 Hope to Prioritize adversary until next rest; can swap Hope and Fear Dice on attacks against them; only 1 at a time

### 3.4 Ranger (SRD page 10-11)

**Domains:** Bone & Sage  
**Starting Evasion:** 12  
**Starting HP:** 6  
**Companion (Beastbound only):**
- Evasion: 10
- Experiences: 2 (+2 each)
- Attack: Damage die d6 + range choice (Melee)
- Can take damage as Stress instead of HP
- Level-up options: Intelligent, Light in Dark, Creature Comfort, Armored, Vicious, Resilient, Bonded, Aware

**Class Feature - Ranger's Focus:**
- Spend Hope, make attack against target
- On success: Target becomes Focus
- **While focused:**
  - Know direction they are in
  - Deal damage → they mark Stress
  - Fail attack → end feature to reroll Duality Dice

**Hope Feature - Hold Them Off (3 Hope):**
- After successful attack with weapon, use same roll against 2 additional adversaries within range

**Subclasses:**

**Beastbound (Agility trait):**
- **Foundation:** Companion (at GM's discretion; when level up, choose level-up option for companion)
- **Specialization:**
  - Expert Training: +1 level-up option for companion
  - Battle-Bonded: When adversary attacks you within companion's Melee range, +2 to Evasion against attack
- **Mastery:**
  - Advanced Training: +2 level-up options for companion
  - Loyal Friend: Once per long rest, when damage would mark companion's last Stress or your last HP, you/companion can rush to side and take damage instead

**Wayfinder (Agility trait):**
- **Foundation:**
  - Ruthless Predator: When making damage roll, mark Stress to gain +1 to Proficiency; when deal Severe damage, adversary marks Stress
  - Path Forward: When traveling to visited place or carrying object that was there, identify shortest path
- **Specialization:**
  - Elusive Predator: When Focus makes attack against you, +2 to Evasion against attack
- **Mastery:**
  - Apex Predator: Before attack roll against Focus, spend Hope; on success, remove Fear from GM's pool

### 3.5 Rogue (SRD page 11)

**Domains:** Grace & Midnight  
**Starting Evasion:** 12  
**Starting HP:** 6  
**Cloaked State:** Enhanced Hidden; remain unseen if stationary when adversary moves to normal sight range; lost after attack or end move in line of sight

**Class Features:**

**Cloaked:**
- When would be Hidden → become Cloaked instead
- Hidden benefits + remain unseen if stationary

**Sneak Attack:**
- When succeed on attack while Cloaked or ally at Melee range of target
- Add `[Tier] d6` to damage roll
  - Tier 1 (Level 1): 1d6
  - Tier 2 (Levels 2-4): 2d6
  - Tier 3 (Levels 5-7): 3d6
  - Tier 4 (Levels 8-10): 4d6

**Hope Feature - Rogue's Dodge (3 Hope):**
- Gain +2 to Evasion until next attack succeeds against you, or next rest (whichever first)

**Subclasses:**

**Nightwalker (Finesse trait):**
- **Foundation:** Shadow Stepper
  - Move into darkness/shadow, mark Stress to disappear and reappear in shadow within Far range
  - Reappear Cloaked
- **Specialization:**
  - Dark Cloud: Spellcast Roll (15); create temporary cloud at Close range; inside can't see out, outside can't see in; you're Cloaked from those blocked by cloud
  - Adrenaline: While Vulnerable, add level to damage rolls
- **Mastery:**
  - Fleeting Shadow: +1 permanent Evasion; Shadow Stepper within Very Far range
  - Vanishing Act: Mark Stress to become Cloaked any time; automatically clear Restrained if have it; remain Cloaked until roll Fear or next rest

**Syndicate (Finesse trait):**
- **Foundation:** Well-Connected
  - When arrive in prominent town/environment, know somebody there
  - Give them name, note usefulness, choose fact (owe favor, ask in exchange, in trouble, used to be together, didn't part well)
- **Specialization:** Contacts Everywhere
  - Once per session: Call on shady contact; choose benefit:
    - Provide 1 handful gold, unique tool, or mundane object needed
    - On next action roll, help provides +3 to Hope/Fear Die result
    - Next damage dealt: Contact snipes from shadows, add 2d8
- **Mastery:** Reliable Backup
  - Use Contacts Everywhere 3x per session; added benefits:
    - When mark 1+ HP, they shield you reducing by 1
    - Presence Roll in conversation: Roll d20 as Hope Die

### 3.6 Seraph (SRD page 12)

  - Devout: Roll Prayer Dice, can roll additional die and discard lowest; use Sparing Touch twice per long rest instead

**Winged Sentinel (Strength trait):**

  - While flying: Mark Stress to pick up/carry creature ~same size or smaller; Spend Hope to deal extra 1d8 damage on successful attack
- **Specialization:** Ethereal Visage
  - While flying: Advantage on Presence Rolls; on Presence Roll success with Hope, can remove Fear from GM's pool instead of gain Hope

  - Ascendant: +4 permanent to Severe damage threshold

**Subclasses:**

- **Specialization:** Natural Evasion
  - When attack succeeds against you, mark Stress and describe element defense
  - Roll d6, add to Evasion against attack
- **Mastery:** Transcendence
  - Once per long rest: Transform into physical element; choose 2 benefits until next rest:
    - +4 to Severe threshold
    - +1 to character trait
    - +1 to Proficiency
    - +2 to Evasion

**Primal Origin (Instinct trait):**
- **Foundation:** Manipulate Magic
  - After cast spell or attack with magic damage weapon, mark Stress to:
    - Extend reach by one range, OR
    - +2 to action roll result, OR
    - Double damage die, OR
    - Hit additional target at range
- **Specialization:** Enchanted Aid
  - When Help Ally with Spellcast Roll: Roll d8 as advantage die
  - Once per long rest: After ally Spellcast Roll with your help, swap results of their Duality Dice
- **Mastery:** Arcane Charge
  - Take magic damage → become Charged (or spend 2 Hope)
  - Successful magic damage attack while Charged: Clear Charge to gain +10 to damage OR +3 to Difficulty of reaction roll spell causes
  - Stop being Charged at next long rest

**Domains:** Blade & Bone  
**Starting Evasion:** 11  
**Starting HP:** 6  

**Class Features:**

  - They can't move
  - Deal weapon damage
  - Move with them

**Combat Training:**

**Hope Feature - No Mercy (3 Hope):**
- +1 to attack rolls until next rest

**Subclasses:**

  - Camaraderie: Initiate Tag Team Roll +1 additional time per session; when ally initiates with you, only costs 2 Hope

**Call of the Slayer (Damage-focused):**
- **Foundation:** Slayer

## SECTION 6: EQUIPMENT & WEAPONS SYSTEM (SRD pages 43-54)

### 6.1 Weapon Attributes

Each weapon has:
- **Tier:** Character level tier allowed
- **Trait:** Which trait for attack roll
- **Range:** Melee / Very Close / Close / Far / Very Far
- **Damage:** Damage die + modifier (e.g., d8+1)
- **Burden:** 1 or 2 hands
- **Damage Type:** Physical or Magic
- **Feature:** Special rule (optional)

### 6.2 Weapon Features (Examples)

- **Reliable:** +1 to attack rolls
- **Quick:** Mark Stress to target additional creature at range
- **Powerful:** Roll additional die, discard lowest
- **Heavy:** −1 to Evasion
- **Massive:** −1 to Evasion; additional damage die, discard lowest
- **Cumbersome:** −1 to Finesse
- **Brutal:** Roll maximum on damage die = roll additional die
- **Deadly:** Deal Severe damage → target marks additional HP
- **Scary:** Successful attack → target marks Stress
- **Versatile:** Can use with alternate statistics
- **Returning:** Thrown weapon returns to hand
- **Reloading:** After attack, roll d6; on 1, mark Stress to reload
- **Paired:** +2 to primary weapon damage within Melee range (secondary weapons)
- **Protective:** +Armor Score (shields)
- **Barrier:** +Armor Score; −Evasion (tower shields)
- **Double Duty:** +Armor Score and +primary weapon damage (shields)
- **Parry:** Roll weapon's damage dice; if matches attacker's, discard matching results

### 6.3 Weapon Tiers & Availability

- **Tier 1 (Level 1):** Primary and Secondary weapons available
- **Tier 2 (Levels 2-4):** Improved versions available
- **Tier 3 (Levels 5-7):** Advanced versions available
- **Tier 4 (Levels 8-10):** Legendary versions available
- **Unarmed:** [Proficiency] d4 damage using Strength or Finesse (GM's choice)

### 7.1 Armor Mechanics

**Armor Score:**
- Indicates number of Armor Slots available
- Can't exceed 12
- Marked slots reduce incoming damage by one threshold
- When unarmored: Base Score = 0, Major threshold = Level, Severe threshold = 2× Level

**Base Thresholds:**
- Major and Severe thresholds (before adding level)
- Final threshold = Base + Level

## SECTION 9: DOWNTIME & REST SYSTEMS (SRD pages 21-22)

### 9.1 Rest Types

**Short Rest:**
- Duration: ~1 hour
- Domain Cards: Can freely swap between loadout/vault
- Downtime Moves: Choose 2 (can repeat)
- Refresh: Features with "per rest" uses refresh
- GMgains Fear: 1d4

**Long Rest:**
- Duration: Several in-game hours
- Domain Cards: Can freely swap between loadout/vault
- Downtime Moves: Choose 2 (can repeat)
- Refresh: Features with "per rest" or "per long rest" uses refresh
- GM gains Fear: 1d4 + number of PCs
- GM can advance one long-term countdown

**Consecutive Short Rests:**
- If 3 short rests in a row, next rest must be long rest

### 9.2 Short Rest Downtime Moves

1. **Tend to Wounds:** Clear 1d4+Tier HP (self or ally)
2. **Clear Stress:** Clear 1d4+Tier Stress (self)
3. **Repair Armor:** Clear 1d4+Tier Armor Slots (self or ally)
4. **Prepare:** Describe preparation, gain 1 Hope (or 2 Hope if with 1+ allies)

### 9.3 Long Rest Downtime Moves

1. **Tend to All Wounds:** Clear all HP (self or ally)
2. **Clear All Stress:** Clear all Stress (self)
3. **Repair All Armor:** Clear all Armor Slots (self or ally)
4. **Prepare:** Describe preparation, gain 1 Hope (or 2 Hope if with 1+ allies)
5. **Work on a Project:** Advance long-term project countdown (automatic or action roll; GM's choice)

## SECTION 10: DEATH & SCAR MECHANICS (SRD page 22)

When PC marks last Hit Point, choose:

### 10.1 Blaze of Glory
- Describe going out dramatically
- Take one final action (auto-critically succeeds with GM approval)
- Then die

### 10.2 Avoid Death
- Character drops unconscious
- Work with GM to describe worsening situation
- While unconscious: Can't move/act, can't be targeted
- Return to consciousness: When ally clears 1+ HP OR after long rest
- **Scar Check:** Roll Hope Die; if result ≤ level, gain scar
  - Cross out Hope slot permanently
  - Work with GM on narrative impact and restoration method
  - If cross out last Hope slot → character's journey ends

### 10.3 Risk It All
- Roll Duality Dice
- Hope Die higher: Stay up, clear HP/Stress equal to Hope Die result (split as desired)
- Fear Die higher: Die
- Matching dice: Stay up, clear all HP and Stress
- If die: Create new character at current party level before next session
